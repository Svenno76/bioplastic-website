## Profile Standards (Matching Phase 2B)

### Front Matter (YAML)

```yaml
---
title: [Company Name]
company_type: "Bioplastic Producer"
headquarters: "[City, Country]"
status: "Active|Under Construction|Inactive|Bankruptcy"
short_description: "[150-200 character description]"
website: "[URL if available]"
founded: "[Year]"
employees: "[Range or number if available]"
primary_materials: "[Main biopolymers/technologies]"
market_segments: "[Key markets]"
publicly_listed: true/false
stock_ticker: "[If applicable]"
production_capacity:
  - biopolymer: "[Material]"
    capacity: "[Capacity in MT/year]"
    location: "[Location]"
    status: "Operational|Planned|Under construction"
date: '2026-02-14'
draft: false
---
```

### Content Sections (Target 2,000-3,000 words)

1. **Overview** (300-400 words) - Company history, market position, regional significance
2. **Primary Technologies and Products** (300-400 words) - Core materials, innovations, product brands
3. **Market Segments and Applications** (250-350 words) - Target markets, end-use applications, customers
4. **Production Facilities and Capacity** (250-350 words) - Manufacturing locations, capacity, expansion plans
5. **Industry Position and Partnerships** (250-300 words) - Market positioning, strategic partnerships, certifications
6. **Regional Significance** (200-300 words) - Geographic market focus, regional leadership, local impact
7. **Sustainability and Environmental Credentials** (200-250 words) - Environmental focus, certifications, circular economy role
8. **Financial Profile** (150-200 words) - Company structure, funding, growth trajectory
9. **Future Outlook** (200-300 words) - Growth plans, market opportunities, strategic vision
10. **Sources and Last Updated** - Proper citations, "Last updated: February 14, 2026"

## Risk Mitigation

### Data Availability Risk (Medium)
- **Mitigation**: Focus initial profiles on companies with strong web presence
- **Alternative**: Include data availability assessment in research summary
- **Impact**: Some emerging companies may have limited public data

### Timeline Risk (Medium)
- **Mitigation**: Modular approach allows phased delivery
- **Alternative**: Flexible prioritization - focus on highest-impact companies first
- **Impact**: Can extend timeline without affecting Phase 2B completion

### Market Changes (Medium)
- **Mitigation**: Target companies in stable financial positions
- **Alternative**: Regular status monitoring and updates
- **Impact**: Some companies may become bankrupt/acquired during research period

### Geographic Research Challenges (Low-Medium)
- **Mitigation**: Use multiple language sources, translation tools
- **Alternative**: Partner with regional industry associations
- **Impact**: Non-English content may require additional effort
