## Phase 2C: Database Expansion Strategy

### Scope Definition

**Target**: 60+ additional regional and emerging bioplastics companies beyond the current 43 Bioplastic Producers

**Selection Criteria**:
- Regional producers with significant market presence in their geography
- Emerging companies with innovative technologies
- Small to mid-size manufacturers (50-500 employees typically)
- Specialty producers serving niche applications
- Vertically integrated companies with feedstock control
- Companies with recent funding or expansion announcements
- Geographic representation: Asia (25), Africa (10), South America (10), Eastern Europe (10), Middle East (5)

### Research Sources for Phase 2C

1. **Industry Databases**:
   - European Bioplastics (EUBP) member directory
   - National bioplastics associations (USA, Japan, South Korea, India, etc.)
   - Industry-specific research reports
   - Investment databases (Crunchbase, PitchBook, etc.)

2. **Geographic Focus Areas**:
   - **Asia-Pacific**: India, China, Southeast Asia (Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam), Malaysia
   - **South America**: Brazil, Argentina, Colombia
   - **Africa**: Kenya, South Africa, Nigeria
   - **Middle East**: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Iran
   - **Eastern Europe**: Poland, Czech Republic, Turkey

3. **Technology Focus Areas**:
   - Emerging feedstock technologies (algae, ocean-based, waste-derived)
   - Enzymatic recycling and upcycling companies
   - Novel polymer chemistries
   - Application-specific bioplastics

### Company Identification Process

1. **Web Research**: Company websites, LinkedIn, industry publications
2. **Patent Analysis**: USPTO, WIPO databases for recent innovations
3. **News Monitoring**: Recent funding announcements, facility openings, partnerships
4. **Industry Events**: EUBP conferences, Interplast, PlasticsNews, etc.
5. **Supplier Networks**: Integration with equipment suppliers and technology partners
