## Overview

Clariant International AG is a major Swiss specialty chemicals company headquartered in Muttenz, near Basel, Switzerland. With a workforce of 10,465 employees distributed across 55 countries and 17 research centers, Clariant is a global leader in specialty chemicals and advanced materials. The company manufactures additives, colorants, pigments, and specialized chemicals serving diverse industries including plastics processing, coatings, textiles, pharmaceuticals, and industrial applications.

Clariant operates through multiple business segments focused on innovation and customer collaboration, developing tailored chemical solutions addressing specific industry needs and supporting circular economy principles.
