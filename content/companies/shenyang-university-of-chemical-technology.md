Shenyang University of Chemical Technology, located in Shenyang, China, is a leading academic institution focused on chemical and materials research. The university collaborates with Northeast Forestry University on developing innovative bamboo cellulose-based bioplastics using advanced non-toxic alcohol solvent processes.

## Research Focus

The university specializes in developing sustainable bioplastic solutions that combine:
- Environmental responsibility with high performance
- Renewable bamboo cellulose as the base material
- Non-toxic processing technologies

## Key Achievements

- Development of bioplastics matching petroleum-based plastic performance
- Complete biodegradability in soil (50 days)
- Strong recyclability (retains 90% strength after recycling)
- Scalable production technology

Bamboo cellulose-based bioplastic
