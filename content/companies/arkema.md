## Overview

Arkema S.A. is a major French specialty chemicals company founded in 2004 as part of Total's restructuring of its chemicals business and listed on the Paris stock exchange in May 2006. Headquartered in La Défense, near Paris, Arkema operates across 55 countries with 21,150 employees and 157 production plants. The company is a global leader in specialty chemicals, innovative materials, and solutions, with particular focus on sustainable and bio-based products.

Arkema is strategically positioned at the intersection of chemical innovation and sustainability, developing advanced materials that support circular economy principles and address global environmental challenges across diverse industrial applications.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Bio-Based Polymers & Materials:**
