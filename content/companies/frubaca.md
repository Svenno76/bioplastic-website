## Overview

Frubaça is a well-established Portuguese beverage producer based in Vila Nova de Gaia (Porto region), with over 30 years of experience in fruit processing and beverage manufacturing. The company specializes in premium fruit juices, functional beverages, and innovative drink formats, with a strong emphasis on natural ingredients, clean label formulations, and sustainable packaging.

Frubaça has built its reputation on authentic Portuguese fruit flavors, leveraging the country's rich agricultural heritage and proximity to high-quality fruit production regions.

### Product Portfolio

- **100% Fruit Juices**: NFC (not from concentrate) and premium concentrate-based juices
- **Functional Beverages**: Vitamin-fortified shots, antioxidant blends, energy drinks
- **Fruit-Based Drinks**: Nectars, still and sparkling fruit beverages
- **Innovative Formats**: Small-format "shot" beverages (30-100ml), on-the-go packaging
- **Private Label**: Contract manufacturing for major retail and brand partners

### Sustainability Commitment

Frubaça has integrated sustainability across its value chain:

- **Sustainable Sourcing**: Direct relationships with Portuguese fruit growers, supporting local agriculture
- **Clean Label**: No artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors; minimal processing
- **Packaging Innovation**: Early adopter of rPET and bio-based packaging trials
- **Carbon Reduction**: Energy efficiency investments and renewable energy procurement
- **Water Stewardship**: Advanced water treatment and recycling in production

### Avantium PEF Collaboration (2026)

In June 2026, Frubaça announced a landmark collaboration with **Avantium** (Netherlands) and **Gepack** (Portugal) to trial **PEF "releaf®" bottles** for functional beverage shots. This project represents:

- **First-mover advantage** in small-format PEF beverage packaging in Portugal
- **Value-chain partnership** spanning polymer producer (Avantium), bottle manufacturer (Gepack), and brand owner/filler (Frubaça)
- **Regulatory alignment** with upcoming EU PPWR recycled content and bio-based content targets
- **Consumer testing** of PEF packaging in real-market conditions

Frubaça handles the formulation, filling, and market launch of the functional beverage shots, while Gepack produces the PEF preforms and bottles, and Avantium supplies the PEF polymer.

### Market Position

Frubaça is a leading mid-sized beverage producer in Portugal, with distribution across:
- Modern retail (supermarkets, hypermarkets)
- Foodservice and hospitality (hotels, restaurants, cafes)
- Health and wellness channels (pharmacies, specialty stores)
- Export markets (Spain, France, Benelux, Nordics)

The company's focus on functional beverages and innovative packaging formats aligns with growing consumer demand for convenient, health-oriented, and sustainably packaged products.
