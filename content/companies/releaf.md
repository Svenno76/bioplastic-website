ReLeaf is a plant-based, recyclable plastic brand by Avantium. It is made from PEF (polyethylene furanoate), a 100% bio-based polymer that serves as a sustainable alternative to conventional PET. PEF offers superior barrier properties for packaging applications and is completely fossil-free. Avantium has developed the YXY technology to produce FDCA (the key building block for PEF) from plant-based sugars.
