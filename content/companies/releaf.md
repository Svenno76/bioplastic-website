## Overview

ReLeaf is the commercial brand for Avantium's PEF (polyethylene furanoate) technology — a breakthrough 100% plant-based, recyclable polyester that serves as a sustainable alternative to conventional PET. Developed by Avantium N.V. using their proprietary YXY® Technology, ReLeaf represents a new generation of bio-based polymers that are both high-performing and environmentally responsible.

PEF is produced from FDCA (furandicarboxylic acid), which Avantium manufactures from plant-based sugars through its catalytic YXY® process. The resulting polymer offers superior performance characteristics compared to PET while being completely fossil-free and compatible with existing recycling infrastructure.

## Core Technology: YXY® Process

### FDCA Production
Avantium's patented YXY® Technology converts plant-based fructose into FDCA (furandicarboxylic acid), the key building block for PEF:

**Process Steps:**
1. **Feedstock**: Plant-based sugars (fructose from starch crops)
2. **Dehydration**: Catalytic conversion to HMF (hydroxymethylfurfural)
3. **Oxidation**: Selective oxidation to FDCA
4. **Purification**: High-purity FDCA suitable for polymerization
5. **Polymerization**: FDCA + monoethylene glycol (MEG) → PEF

### PEF Polymer Properties

**Superior Barrier Performance:**
- **10× better oxygen barrier** than PET
- **2× better CO₂ barrier** than PET
- **Excellent water vapor barrier**
- Enables extended shelf life for sensitive products

**Mechanical Advantages:**
- Higher tensile strength than PET
- Improved thermal stability
- Better processability on existing PET equipment
- Allows lightweighting (thinner walls, less material)

**Sustainability Profile:**
- **100% bio-based** (when using bio-based MEG)
- **Fully recyclable** in existing PET recycling streams
- **Lower carbon footprint** vs. fossil-based PET
- **No food contact migration issues**

## Environmental Benefits

### Lifecycle Assessment
- **~50-70% lower GHG emissions** vs. fossil PET (depending on MEG source)
- **Bio-based carbon content**: Up to 100% (with bio-MEG)
- **Circularity**: Recyclable in existing infrastructure
- **Non-toxic**: No antimony catalysts, no acetaldehyde migration

### End-of-Life
- **Mechanical recycling**: Compatible with PET recycling (validated)
- **Chemical recycling**: Depolymerization to monomers possible
- **Biodegradation**: Not compostable (durable polymer), but recyclable
- **No microplastic persistence**: Recyclable circular pathway
