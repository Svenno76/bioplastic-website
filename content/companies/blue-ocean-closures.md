## Overview

Blue Ocean Closures is a Swedish technology company founded in late 2021 with headquarters in Karlstad and production facilities in Säffle. The company focuses on developing and manufacturing advanced fiber-based closure systems using cellulose fibers from sustainably managed forests as sustainable alternatives to conventional plastic closures. BOC's proprietary vacuum press forming technology enables rapid, high-volume production of closures with significantly reduced environmental impact.

## Core Technology: Vacuum Press Forming

**Process Advantages**:
- **Speed**: Cycle times down to 1.8 seconds enabling high-volume production
- **Material**: Cellulose fibers from sustainably managed forests
- **Additives**: Food-safe strength and hydrophobicity additives used in other paper packaging
- **Recyclability**: Products designed for recycling as paper-based materials

## Product Lines

**BOC 38-400 Cap**:

## Environmental Benefits

- **Carbon Footprint**: Reduction up to 9 times compared to fossil plastic closures
- **Material Source**: Sustainably managed forest resources
- **End-of-Life**: Recyclable as paper-based materials
- **Biodegradable**: Fully compostable in industrial composting facilities
- **Circular Design**: Designed for material loop closure
