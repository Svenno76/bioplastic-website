## Overview

DuPont is an American multinational advanced materials and specialty chemicals company headquartered in Wilmington, Delaware. With a history dating back to 1802 and over 30,000 employees worldwide, DuPont is a global leader in developing innovative materials and chemical solutions serving diverse industries. The company specializes in advanced polymers, specialty chemicals, and engineered materials addressing complex customer needs across electronics, automotive, construction, healthcare, and industrial sectors.

DuPont has a strong heritage of innovation in bioplastics through its Sorona biopolymer development and previous biomaterials initiatives, positioning the company as a contributor to sustainable material solutions.

### Sorona Biopolymer Technology

**Sorona Heritage:**
DuPont's Sorona represented a major innovation in bio-based polymers:
- Proprietary bio-derived polymer from corn-based lactic acid
- Reduced carbon footprint compared to conventional polymers
- Performance properties comparable to traditional plastics
- Applications in fibers, films, and injection molding

**Recent Developments:**
In October 2020, DuPont announced the sale of its biomaterial business to Huafon Group for $240 million. The Kinston, North Carolina facility continues operations under the new ownership as Covation Biomaterials, supporting ongoing development of bio-based polymer technology.
