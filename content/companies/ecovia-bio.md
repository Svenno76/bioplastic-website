## Overview

Ecovia Bio is a Michigan-based biotechnology company and spinout from the University of Michigan focused on the development and commercialization of sustainable, high-performance biopolymers. The company specializes in gamma polyglutamic acid (γ-PGA), a natural biodegradable polymer derived from fermentation processes, offering a transformative alternative to persistent synthetic polymers in cosmetics, personal care, agriculture, and hygiene applications.

Recent Series B funding will enable significant expansion of manufacturing capacity and position the company to serve surging global demand for biodegradable biopolymer solutions.

### Core Technology: Gamma Polyglutamic Acid (γ-PGA)

**Material Properties:**
- 100% biobased, biodegradable polymer
- Produced through fermentation processes from renewable resources
- Fully biodegradable at end of life
- High-performance properties equivalent to or superior to synthetic alternatives
- Non-toxic and environmentally safe
- Suitable for skin contact and sensitive applications

**Key Advantages:**
- Replaces persistent synthetic polymers (e.g., polyacrylamide, sodium polyacrylate)
- No microplastic residue or environmental accumulation
- Delivers equivalent or superior performance
- Supports circular economy and sustainable product development

### Product Portfolio

**AzuraBase® Family:**
- Biopolymer base materials for cosmetics formulation
- Applications: Skincare creams, serums, and moisturizers
- Benefits: Natural origin, biodegradable, high efficacy

**AzuraGel® Family:**
- Specialized gel and suspension materials
- Applications: Personal care products (shampoos, body washes, gels)
- Benefits: Superior texture, stability, and performance

### Market Applications

**Cosmetics & Personal Care:**
- Skincare products (creams, serums, masks)
- Hair care (shampoos, conditioners)
- Body care (gels, lotions)
- Color cosmetics and makeup applications
- Premium and natural beauty brands

**Agriculture:**
- Soil conditioning and water retention
- Sustainable crop protection solutions
- Biodegradable agricultural films

**Hygiene Products:**
- Superabsorbent applications (diapers, feminine hygiene)
- Replaces petroleum-based superabsorbent polymers (SAPs)
- Fully biodegradable alternative to synthetic SAPs

**Industrial Applications:**
- Specialty coatings and binders
- Functional additives for sustainable manufacturing
