## Overview

TreeToTextile AB is a Stockholm-based clean technology company founded in 2014, specializing in next-generation cellulose fiber technology. The company has developed a proprietary process for producing regenerated cellulose fibers from sustainably managed forests with dramatically reduced environmental impact — achieving 70-90% lower carbon emissions compared to conventional viscose and cotton production.

In a significant strategic move, Lenzing AG (the world's leading cellulose fiber producer with TENCEL® and VEOCEL™ brands) acquired a majority stake in TreeToTextile, validating the technology's commercial potential and providing a clear pathway to industrial-scale deployment.

## Core Technology

### Sustainable Fiber Production Process

**Breakthrough Innovation:**
TreeToTextile's patented process represents a fundamental advancement in man-made cellulosic fiber production, addressing the key environmental challenges of traditional viscose manufacturing:

**Process Advantages:**
- **Closed-loop solvent system**: Non-toxic, recyclable solvent with >99% recovery rate
- **Minimal water consumption**: Up to 95% less water than cotton cultivation
- **Chemical-free processing**: Eliminates carbon disulfide (CS₂) used in traditional viscose
- **Energy efficiency**: Low-temperature process reducing energy demand
- **Forest-to-fiber traceability**: Full chain of custody from certified sustainable forests

**Fiber Properties:**
- High tensile strength comparable to conventional viscose
- Excellent moisture management and breathability
- Natural biodegradability in soil and marine environments
- Versatile dyeing compatibility with existing textile infrastructure
- Suitable for both woven and knitted applications

## Environmental Impact

### Carbon Footprint Reduction
- **70-90% lower GHG emissions** vs. conventional viscose
- **Significant reduction** vs. cotton (which requires irrigation, pesticides, land)
- **Renewable feedstock**: FSC/PEFC certified wood from sustainably managed forests
- **Circular potential**: Fibers designed for textile-to-textile recycling

### Resource Efficiency
- **Land use**: 1/10th the land area vs. cotton per kg of fiber
- **Water use**: <50 liters/kg vs. 10,000+ liters/kg for cotton
- **Chemical elimination**: No CS₂, no heavy metals, no toxic byproducts
