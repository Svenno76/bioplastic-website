## Overview

Loliware Inc. is an award-winning materials technology company specializing in innovative seaweed-derived bioplastics and edible packaging solutions. Founded in the 2010s and headquartered in New York, Loliware has pioneered proprietary SEA Technology™ for converting seaweed polymers into food-safe, home-compostable, and even edible packaging materials that match conventional plastic performance while delivering genuine environmental benefits and supporting carbon negative production at scale.

The company's distinctive approach combines seaweed polymer science with innovative product development, creating not only compostable alternatives to conventional plastics but also edible packaging that can be consumed with products or safely decompose in home compost environments. This represents a paradigm shift in sustainable packaging, offering end-consumers actual choices beyond traditional "throw away" solutions.

### Proprietary SEA Technology™

**Seaweed Polymer Innovation**:
- Extraction and processing of seaweed-derived polymers (alginate and agar compounds)
- Conversion into food-contact-compliant, compostable resins
- Processing into sheets, films, and finished products
- Formulation for edibility and food safety
- Patent-protected proprietary technology platform

**Key Technology Characteristics**:
- 100% natural seaweed feedstock
- Home-compostable formulations (complete degradation in residential composting)
- Edible variants suitable for direct consumption
- Ocean-safe degradation profile
- Processing compatibility with existing manufacturing equipment
- Food contact approved materials

**Product Development**:
- Blue Ocean Straws™: Edible seaweed straws for beverages
- Cocktail Straws: Flavored seaweed straws for bar and hospitality use
- SEA Technology Pellets: Resin feedstock for converter manufacturing
- Custom formulations for specific applications
- Transparent and opaque variants
