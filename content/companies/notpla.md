## Overview

Notpla is a London-based sustainable packaging innovator founded in 2015, specializing in revolutionary 100% natural seaweed-based coatings for food contact and packaging applications. The company has pioneered a unique approach using non-chemically modified seaweed as the primary material for compostable food service packaging, achieving environmental credentials that exceed conventional bioplastics through complete naturalness and rapid biodegradation.

Notpla's commitment to 100% natural materials—rejecting synthetic additives even when chemically derived from renewable sources—positions it as the most environmentally conservative player in the seaweed-based bioplastics segment, appealing to consumers and brands prioritizing genuine natural materials over plant-derived synthetics.

### Seaweed-Based Technology

**100% Natural Seaweed Coating**:
- Non-chemically modified seaweed material
- Direct use of seaweed as biopolymer source
- No synthetic additives or chemical processing
- Truly natural and ocean-derived material
- Unique differentiation in bioplastics market

**Key Characteristics**:
- Completely compostable within 6 weeks (home compostable)
- No microplastics or persistent residues
- PFAS-free composition (critical for food contact)
- Ocean-safe and marine-friendly
- Food contact compliant (verified through testing)

**Compostability Standards**:
- EU Single-Use Plastic Directive compliant
- Home composting verified (6-week degradation)
- Industrial composting capable
- Verified natural degradation (no microplastics)
- Natural biological decomposition
