## Overview

Plastipol is a Polish plastics processing and compounding company headquartered in Szczecin, Poland, specializing in the development and production of biodegradable and recycled plastic products. Founded in 2000, the company has established itself as a specialized processor and compounder of thermoplastic materials, working closely with clients to select optimal materials and production technologies for their specific applications. Plastipol's core business centers on transforming conventional and bio-based polymers into finished and semi-finished products through advanced processing technologies.

The company's location in Szczecin, a major industrial port city in northwestern Poland, provides strategic advantages for accessing raw materials and distributing products throughout European markets. Plastipol operates as a technology-driven processor, offering consulting services to help customers navigate the increasingly complex landscape of material selection between conventional and biodegradable alternatives.

## Processing Technologies and Capabilities

**Injection Molding**
Plastipol operates advanced injection molding equipment for precision manufacturing of plastic components, supporting production runs from prototypes to high-volume commercial orders. The technology is applicable to both conventional and biodegradable thermoplastics.

**Thermoforming**
Specialized thermoforming capabilities enable the creation of complex, thin-walled plastic components from biodegradable and conventional materials. This technology is particularly suitable for packaging applications and consumer products.

**Rotational Casting**
Advanced rotational molding technology supports the manufacturing of hollow plastic components with uniform wall thickness, ideal for larger containers and specialized applications using bio-based materials.

**Extrusion Processing**
Plastic extrusion capabilities enable the production of plastic films, tubes, and profiles from various thermoplastic materials, including biodegradable alternatives to conventional plastics.

**3D Printing**
Plastipol offers advanced 3D printing services for rapid prototyping and custom component production, supporting both traditional and bio-based polymer materials for design validation and small-batch manufacturing.
