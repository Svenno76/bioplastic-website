## Overview

SK Chemicals is a major South Korean chemical company headquartered in Seoul and founded in 1969, establishing over 55 years of chemical industry expertise and innovation. Publicly listed on the Korea Exchange (KRX:011170), the company has positioned itself as a global leader in biodegradable polymers and bio-based chemical solutions. Recognized as world's No. 2 copolyester producer alongside Mitsubishi Chemical, SK Chemicals has built a comprehensive portfolio of bioplastics including PLA, BioPBS, and its innovative ECOTRION (PO3G biopolyol) technology.

The company's strategic commitment to biodegradable materials, bio-based chemistry, and advanced recycling solutions positions SK Chemicals at the forefront of the circular bioeconomy transition globally.

### Core Bioplastic Technologies

**PLA (Polylactic Acid)**:
- Pioneer in PLA development and commercialization
- Market-proven product portfolio
- Diverse grades for multiple applications
- Industrial and home composting capable
- Food contact compliant formulations

**BioPBS (Bio-Based Polybutylene Succinate)**:
- Biodegradable polyester from renewable feedstocks
- Industrial composting certified
- Soil and water degradable
- Customizable for specific applications
- Performance comparable to conventional polyesters

**ECOTRION (PO3G Biopolyol)**:
- **Innovative breakthrough technology**
- 100% bio-based from fermented plants
- Polyol for polyurethane applications
- Thermoplastic and flexible foam uses
- Mass production demonstrates commercialization success
- Reduces petroleum dependence in polyurethane industry

### ECOTRION Technology Significance

**PO3G Biopolyol Innovation**:
- Propane-1,3-diol (PO3G) derived from plant fermentation
- Converts plant sugars to bio-based chemical building block
- Enables replacement of petroleum-based polyols
- Polyurethane foam production without fossil fuels
- Applications in mattresses, cushioning, flexible foam

**Market Impact**:
- Enables bio-based polyurethane foam production
- Significantly reduces carbon footprint of cushioning materials
- BPA-free composition
- Thermal properties suitable for comfort applications
- Mass production proving commercial viability
