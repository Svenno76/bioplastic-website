## Overview

Saudi Basic Industries Corporation (SABIC) is a global leader in chemical and plastics manufacturing, founded in 1976 by royal decree to convert oil by-products into useful chemicals, polymers, and fertilizers. Headquartered in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, SABIC operates as a major petrochemical and polymer producer with a global workforce exceeding 28,000 employees across more than 50 countries and serving customers in 140+ nations worldwide.

SABIC has evolved from a petrochemical-focused company to a diversified chemical and materials producer actively developing sustainable solutions, including bio-based polymers and renewable plastics, reflecting growing market demands for environmentally responsible materials.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Bio-Based Polyolefins:**
- **Bio-PE (Bio-Polyethylene)**: Drop-in replacement polyethylene derived from renewable feedstocks
- **Bio-PP (Bio-Polypropylene)**: Bio-based polypropylene for diverse applications
- Certified renewable polymer content products
- Mixed bio-based/conventional polyolefin blends

**Engineering Plastics & Specialties:**
- Polycarbonate (PC) and blends
- Polyether ether ketone (PEEK)
- Specialty polyamides (PA)
- Advanced thermoplastics for demanding applications
- Polyurethane precursors and systems

**Core Polymer Portfolio:**
- Commodity polyethylene and polypropylene
- Specialty chemicals and additives
- Fertilizer and agri-nutrient products
- Industrial chemicals

### Market Segments

SABIC serves diverse global industries with tailored material solutions:
- **Packaging**: Films, containers, and protective packaging for food and industrial applications
- **Automotive**: Interior and exterior components, lightweighting solutions
- **Electrical & Electronics**: High-performance polymers for electronic devices and components
- **Medical Devices**: Biocompatible materials for healthcare applications
- **Construction & Building**: Durable materials for structural and insulation applications
- **Agriculture**: Specialized agri-nutrients and crop solutions
- **Industrial Applications**: Specialty chemicals for manufacturing processes

### Sustainable Materials Initiative

SABIC is committed to developing bio-based and circular economy solutions:
- **Bio-Polyol Technology**: Renewable polyol feedstocks from sustainable sources
- **Recycled Content Programs**: Integration of post-consumer and post-industrial recycled plastics
- **Renewable Feedstock Development**: Partnerships for sustainable raw material sourcing
- **Circular Design Principles**: Product development focusing on recyclability and lifecycle reduction

### Global Operations & Facilities

SABIC operates multiple world-scale manufacturing complexes across:
- **Middle East** (primary operations hub)
- **Europe** (manufacturing and R&D centers)
- **North America** (production and technical support)
- **Asia-Pacific** (regional manufacturing and distribution)

The company maintains extensive research and development capabilities focused on innovative materials, process optimization, and sustainable chemistry solutions.

### Strategic Partnerships

SABIC collaborates with leading companies for bio-based material development, including partnerships for bio-derived polymer production. For example, SABIC supplies Bio-PP to UPM for their Formi EcoAce brand biocomposite used in food contact, personal care, and consumer goods applications.
