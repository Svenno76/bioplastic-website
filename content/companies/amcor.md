## Overview

Amcor plc is the world's largest packaging company headquartered in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. With 77,000 employees and operations across the globe, Amcor is a market leader in flexible and rigid packaging solutions serving diverse industries. Operating across two main segments (Flexibles and Rigid Packaging), the company develops innovative, sustainable, and value-adding packaging solutions while meeting the highest standards for product protection and environmental responsibility.

Amcor is strategically positioned as a global packaging innovator, investing significantly in sustainable materials including bioplastics, recycled content products, and advanced packaging technologies to support customers' environmental goals and emerging market demands for sustainable packaging.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Bioplastics & Sustainable Materials:**
- **PLA (Polylactic Acid)**: Bio-based, compostable polymer for packaging applications
- **Bio-PE (Biobased Polyethylene)**: Renewable polyethylene for flexible packaging
- **Recycled Polymers**: Post-consumer and post-industrial recycled materials
- **Compostable Packaging Solutions**: Certified sustainable alternatives
- **Advanced Bio-Derived Compounds**: Innovative bio-based material formulations

**Flexible Packaging Products:**
- Films and pouches for food and beverage products
- Protective flexible packaging

- Custom formulations for specific applications

**Rigid Packaging Products:**

- Closures and caps

**Materials Portfolio:**

### Market Segments

Amcor serves diverse global industries with comprehensive packaging solutions:
- **Food & Beverage**: Flexible and rigid packaging for food products, beverages, and condiments

- **Personal Care & Cosmetics**: Packaging for beauty, personal care, and toiletry products
