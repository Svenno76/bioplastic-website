# Lubrizol Corporation

Lubrizol is a leading specialty chemical company delivering science-based innovations that enhance life, provide local solutions, and accelerate progress across diverse industries including personal care, transportation, and industrial applications.

## Overview

Founded in 1928, Lubrizol has established itself as a pioneer in specialty chemicals with a century-long commitment to innovation. The company operates globally, combining strategic research with market insights to develop sustainable solutions that address global challenges. Lubrizol is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway.

## Products

### Biodegradable Polymer Portfolio

#### Carbopol® BioSense Polymer
A readily biodegradable rheology modifier and sensory enhancer for personal care applications:
- **Sourcing**: Responsibly sourced from eucalyptus
- **Performance**: Mimics the luxurious feel of silicone elastomers
- **Sustainability**: 98% natural origin content
- **Certifications**: COSMOS/ECOCERT certified, readily biodegradable (OECD 301D)
- **Benefits**: Enhances viscosity while providing rich, soft, non-sticky sensory experience

#### Sensomer™ Cassia 115 Polymer
A naturally derived, biodegradable conditioning polymer for hair and skin care:
- **Sourcing**: Derived from cassia plant
- **Applications**: Both rinse-off and leave-on formulations
- **Properties**: Excellent sensory profile, superior deposition, foam-boosting capabilities
- **Characteristics**: Vegan-suitable, non-GMO, inherently biodegradable (OECD 301B), microbiome-friendly
- **Performance**: Outperforms traditional benchmarks in cationic and silicone deposition

### Application Areas
- Personal care and cosmetics
- Industrial lubricants and additives
- Transportation fuel additives
- Coatings and inks

## Innovation and Sustainability

Lubrizol's strategic focus on sustainable solutions responds to growing market demand for environmentally responsible ingredients. The company's biodegradable polymer innovations enable industries to develop sustainable formulations without compromising product quality or consumer experience.

### Research and Development
Lubrizol maintains a strong commitment to science-based innovation, continuously developing high-performance alternatives that align with environmental responsibility. The 2025 product launches of Carbopol® BioSense and Sensomer™ Cassia 115 polymers exemplify this commitment to advancing sustainable chemistry.

## Market Position

With a global presence and diverse product portfolio, Lubrizol serves customers across multiple industries, empowering them to create more sustainable products that meet evolving consumer expectations and regulatory requirements. The company's century of experience combined with forward-thinking innovation positions it as a trusted partner in the transition to more sustainable chemical solutions.
