## Overview

EcoPHA Biotech is a biotechnology company specializing in the production of PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoate) bioplastics from pongamia oil via microbial fermentation, offering a fully biodegradable alternative to petroleum-based plastics. In partnership with Terra Sol Studio, an Australian design studio, they develop and commercialize PHA-based products such as compostable coffee cups, lids, straws, cutlery, bags, and materials for industrial applications.
