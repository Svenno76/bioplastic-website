## Overview

FKuR Kunststoff GmbH, based in Willich, Germany, is a leading compounder specializing in bioplastics and biodegradable polymer compounds. Founded in 1998, FKuR has established itself as a reliable partner for customers seeking high-quality bioplastic compounds for a wide range of applications.

The company's product portfolio includes several well-known bioplastic compound families, each designed for specific processing methods and end-use applications. FKuR's compounds are used by plastic converters worldwide to produce sustainable products that meet both performance requirements and environmental standards.

## Core Product Lines

### Bio-Flex® (PLA Compounds)

Bio-Flex® is FKuR's flagship range of PLA (polylactic acid) compounds, offering a balance of stiffness, transparency, and processability. The Bio-Flex® family includes:

- **Bio-Flex® F 1130**: High-flow grade for thin-wall injection molding
- **Bio-Flex® F 2110**: Flexible grade for film extrusion and blow molding
- **Bio-Flex® F 2201**: Clear grade for transparent film applications
- **Bio-Flex® S 5630**: Semi-rigid grade for thermoforming
- **Bio-Flex® A 4100**: Impact-modified grade for durable applications

Bio-Flex® compounds are certified compostable to EN 13432 and are suitable for food-contact applications where regulations permit.

### Terralene® (Bio-Based PE Compounds)

Terralene® is FKuR's range of bio-based polyethylene compounds produced from sugarcane-derived ethanol. These compounds offer:

- **Drop-in replacement**: Chemically identical to fossil-based PE
- **Renewable content**: Up to 95% bio-based carbon
- **Recyclable**: Compatible with existing PE recycling streams
- **Versatile**: Available in various grades for different processing methods

Terralene® compounds are used in applications where the durability and recyclability of polyethylene are required, but a reduced carbon footprint is desired.

### Bio-Nature (Starch-Based Compounds)

FKuR's Bio-Nature product line comprises thermoplastic starch (TPS) compounds that offer high renewable content and cost-effective biodegradability. These compounds are used primarily in:

- Bags and sacks
- Agricultural films
- Disposable packaging
- Protective packaging

### PHA Compounds

FKuR offers compounds based on PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates), a family of biopolyesters produced by microbial fermentation. PHA compounds offer:

- **Marine biodegradability**: Biodegrades in seawater environments
- **Wide property range**: From rigid to flexible depending on PHA type
- **Bio-based**: 100% produced from renewable resources
- **Compostable**: Certified to industrial and home composting standards

## Technology and Manufacturing

### Compounding Capabilities

FKuR operates state-of-the-art compounding lines capable of processing a wide range of bioplastic materials. The company's compounding expertise includes:

- **Twin-screw compounding**: Precise control of temperature, shear, and mixing
- **Multi-component blending**: Combining polymers, additives, and fillers
- **Reactive extrusion**: In-situ modification for improved compatibility
- **Quality assurance**: Comprehensive testing of all production batches

### Application Development

FKuR provides technical support to customers throughout the product development process, from material selection and formulation development to processing optimization and troubleshooting. The company's technical service team works closely with customers to ensure successful implementation of bioplastic compounds in their products.
