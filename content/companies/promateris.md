## Overview

Promateris is Romania's leading manufacturer of biodegradable and compostable packaging, headquartered in Buftea, Ilfov County, near Bucharest. Originally founded in 1957 as Prodplast, a traditional plastic films and bags manufacturer, the company underwent strategic rebranding as Promateris in 2020 to emphasize its commitment to sustainable, bio-based packaging solutions and circular economy principles.

With 132 employees and modern production facilities totaling over 5,500 square meters of manufacturing space plus 900 square meters of administrative offices, Promateris has positioned itself as the leading compostable packaging producer in Central and Eastern Europe. The company is publicly listed on the Bucharest Stock Exchange (ticker: PPL / ROPPL), providing transparency and access to capital markets. Promateris represents the successful transformation of a traditional packaging manufacturer into a sustainability-focused bioplastics producer serving expanding European markets for certified compostable solutions.

## Company History and Transformation

**Prodplast Heritage (1957-2020)**
Prodplast was established in 1957 as a traditional plastic films and bags manufacturer in Romania, serving domestic and export markets with conventional polyethylene and polypropylene products. For over six decades, the company built expertise in plastic film processing, bag manufacturing, and industrial packaging solutions.

**2015 Strategic Initiative**
In 2015, Prodplast made the strategic decision to diversify into biodegradable and compostable packaging, recognizing both regulatory trends and growing market demand for sustainable alternatives. The company began production of corn starch-based compostable materials, initially financed through Innovation Norway grants and development partnerships.

**2020 Brand Transformation**
In 2020, the company formally rebranded as Promateris to reflect its strategic focus on bio-based and compostable packaging solutions. This rebranding represented not just a name change but a fundamental repositioning toward sustainability and circular economy participation.

**2023-2024 Expansion Phase**
Promateris expanded production capacity, invested in modern manufacturing equipment, and strengthened its position as the leading compostable packaging provider in Central and Eastern Europe through distribution partnerships and customer acquisitions.

## Primary Materials and Products

**Corn Starch-Based Compostable Polymers**
Promateris manufactures compostable films and bags from corn starch and other plant-based resources, including:
- Raw material for film production starting from corn starch
- Conversion into flexible films suitable for bag production
- 100% biodegradable and compostable formulations
- EN 13432 certified for industrial composting
- OK Home Compost certified where applicable

**PLA and PBAT Blends**
- Polylactic Acid (PLA) based materials for compostable applications
- Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate (PBAT) components
- Optimized blends for specific durability and performance requirements
- Combinations providing balance between processing characteristics and end-of-life performance

**Specialized Compostable Films**
- Bio-waste collection bags certified for composting facilities
- T-shirt bags for retail and consumer applications
- Patch handle bags for various packaging needs
- Agricultural films for mulching and crop applications
- Custom formulations for specific customer requirements
