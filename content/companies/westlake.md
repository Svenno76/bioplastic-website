## Overview

Westlake Corporation is a major American diversified manufacturer headquartered in Houston, Texas. Formed in 2017, the company operates as a leading producer of polyvinyl chloride (PVC), specialty polymers, chemicals, and advanced materials. With approximately 15,920 employees, Westlake serves diverse industrial sectors including construction, building materials, specialty polymers, and chemical manufacturing.

The company manufactures a broad range of products and materials serving residential, commercial, and industrial markets, with emphasis on reliable supply, product quality, and operational efficiency. While historically focused on conventional polymers and chemicals, Westlake is increasingly developing advanced material solutions to meet evolving market demands.
