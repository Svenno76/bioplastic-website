## Overview

Moviplast is a Romanian plastic films and bags manufacturer established in 1993, based in the greater Bucharest area. Over three decades, the company has grown from a regional plastic bag producer into one of Romania's leading flexible packaging manufacturers with over 100 employees and annual processing capacity exceeding 2,300 tons of polyethylene and polypropylene materials.

The company's strategic pivot toward biodegradable and compostable packaging represents a significant evolution, reflecting both regulatory trends and market demand for sustainable alternatives. Moviplast now produces a comprehensive range of biodegradable bags certified to EN 13432, alongside conventional and oxobiodegradable plastic products, serving customers across Central Europe, Western Europe, and Nordic markets.

## Company History and Evolution

**Foundation and Early Growth (1993-2003)**
Moviplast was established on September 2, 1993, as SC Moviplast SRL, entering Romania's plastic packaging sector during the early post-communist period. The company rapidly established itself as a quality producer of plastic films and bags, focusing on customer service and product reliability in a developing market.

**Expansion and Certification (2003-2015)**
The company demonstrated commitment to quality and environmental responsibility through:
- **1997**: ISO 9001 certification recognizing quality management systems
- **2003**: ISO 14001 environmental management certification
- **2005**: OHSAS 18001 occupational health and safety certification
- **2015**: Relocation to modern facility with over 5,500 square meters of production space

**Sustainability Transition (2015-Present)**
Beginning in 2015, Moviplast expanded beyond conventional plastics to include significant production of biodegradable and compostable packaging:
- **2018**: Recertification to current ISO standards (ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018)
- **2018**: COFACE Romania EXCELLENT SME ROMANIA certification, renewed through 2021
- **2020-Present**: Significant expansion of biodegradable product portfolio
- **2023-2024**: Continued innovation in sustainable packaging solutions
