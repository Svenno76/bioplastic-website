## Overview

FKuR is a German bioplastics company headquartered in Willich and founded in 2003 through an innovative collaboration between private industry and the Fraunhofer Institute UMSICHT, Germany's leading research organization for sustainability and environmental technologies. The company has established itself as a comprehensive bioplastics specialist with 20+ years of continuous development experience and global manufacturing presence across Europe, North America, and Asia.

FKuR operates extensive facilities in Willich (7,000+ m²) and maintains strategic geographic presence through operations in Cedar Park, Texas (USA) and a joint venture in Pune, India (SKYi FKuR). The company's diverse portfolio of biocompatible and biodegradable bioplastic compounds serves global markets with both standard and specialized formulations.
