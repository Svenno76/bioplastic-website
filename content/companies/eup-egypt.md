## Overview

EuP Egypt is North Africa's emerging leader in biodegradable and compostable bioplastics production, representing Egypt's entry point into the continental bioplastics market. Operating with sophisticated manufacturing capabilities and leveraging Egypt's abundant mineral resources, EuP Egypt produces BiONext branded bioplastic compounds designed to replace conventional plastics in diverse applications while supporting environmental remediation and circular economy principles.

Founded in 2007, EuP Egypt has leveraged over 17 years of global manufacturing expertise to establish itself as a significant bioplastics producer with production capacity of 180,000 tonnes annually. The company combines technical expertise in polymer compound formulation with access to Egypt's high-purity calcium carbonate resources, creating competitive advantages in bioplastics compound production at scale.

Located strategically in Egypt, EuP Egypt serves North African and broader African markets, positioning itself at the nexus of the Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, and African economic zones. The company's significance extends beyond current bioplastics focus: Egypt's agricultural resources (sugar production, agricultural feedstocks), growing regulatory interest in sustainable materials, and strategic geographic position establish EuP Egypt as a potential linchpin in continental bioplastics sector development.
