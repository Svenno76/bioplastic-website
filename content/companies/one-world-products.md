## Overview

One World Products is a bioplastics and sustainable materials company transitioning to rebrand as **Isiah Enterprises** under the leadership of NBA legend entrepreneur Isiah Thomas. Based in Midland, Michigan, and publicly traded on OTC Markets (OTC:OWPC), the company has acquired Eco Bio Plastics and specializes in plant-based compostable bioplastics and advanced biocomposites from renewable feedstocks.

The company represents an innovative convergence of sports entrepreneurship and advanced materials manufacturing, with strategic focus on automotive and packaging applications. Through proprietary processing and compounding technology, the company converts biomass, recycled materials, and renewable feedstocks into high-performance bioplastics and composite materials.

### Company Transition and Rebranding

**One World Products Heritage**: The company originated as One World Products, Inc., an enterprise focused on sustainable business solutions and social impact.

**Isiah Thomas Leadership**: Under the leadership of NBA legend and entrepreneur Isiah Thomas, the company has transitioned to focus on advanced materials manufacturing and bioplastics innovation.

**Eco Bio Plastics Acquisition**: Strategic acquisition of Eco Bio Plastics strengthened the company's capabilities in compostable bioplastic formulation, manufacturing, and commercial relationships.

**Rebranding Strategy**: The transition to Isiah Enterprises reflects the company's evolution toward advanced materials innovation and market leadership under entrepreneurial vision.
