## Overview

Trinseo S.A. is a specialty polymers and bioplastics manufacturer headquartered in Berwyn, Pennsylvania, USA. Founded in 2010, the company has established itself as a leading provider of thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), thermoplastic urethane (TPU), and advanced bioplastics. With approximately 3,400 employees distributed across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, Trinseo serves global customers in footwear and fashion, packaging, automotive, and industrial applications.

The company is strategically positioned in the specialty materials market, offering customized polymer solutions that combine superior performance with sustainability considerations. Trinseo's portfolio includes bio-based and compostable polymers designed to meet evolving customer demands for environmentally responsible materials without compromising performance.
