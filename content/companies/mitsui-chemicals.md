## Overview

Mitsui Chemicals, Inc. is a major Japanese chemical company founded in 1955 and headquartered in Tokyo. With a global workforce of over 19,000 employees, Mitsui Chemicals operates worldwide in the development, manufacturing, and marketing of chemical intermediates, functional plastics, and specialized materials.

The company is strategically positioned in the global bioplastics and sustainable materials market, providing innovative solutions for diverse industrial sectors. Mitsui Chemicals combines traditional chemical expertise with modern biotechnology to develop next-generation materials meeting evolving market demands for sustainability and performance.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Performance Materials Division:**
- Polyolefin-based compounds and functional plastics
- Engineering polymers with specialized properties
- Advanced chemical intermediates

**Functional Polymeric Materials:**
- Bio-based and renewable chemical compounds
- High-performance polymers for specialized applications
- Thermoplastic elastomers and composite materials
- Functional additives and modifiers

**Bio-based Product Development:**
Mitsui Chemicals is actively developing bio-based chemical products and bioplastics as part of its strategic commitment to sustainable materials. The company leverages biotechnology and green chemistry principles to reduce environmental impact while maintaining superior material properties.

### Market Segments

The company serves diverse industrial sectors with tailored material solutions:
- **Automotive**: Lightweight materials, interior components, exterior trims
- **Electronics**: High-performance polymers for electronic components and devices
- **Packaging**: Sustainable packaging materials and protective films
- **Healthcare & Medical**: Biocompatible and specialty polymers for medical applications
- **Consumer Goods**: Materials for household products and consumer appliances
- **Industrial**: Specialized compounds for machinery and equipment manufacturing

### Business Operations

Mitsui Chemicals operates through a portfolio of specialized business units focusing on:
- Raw material sourcing and optimization
- Advanced polymer synthesis and processing
- Application development and market-specific customization
- Sustainability and circular economy initiatives

The company maintains research and development facilities across Japan, Europe, and North America, enabling rapid innovation and market responsiveness.

### Sustainability & Innovation

Mitsui Chemicals is committed to circular economy principles and sustainable development. The company invests significantly in research and development of bio-based materials, renewable chemical production, and waste reduction initiatives. Strategic partnerships with universities and research institutions support ongoing innovation in bioplastics and advanced materials.
