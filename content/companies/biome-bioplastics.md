## Overview

Biome Bioplastics is a UK-based developer of biodegradable and bio-based plastics headquartered in Southampton. Building on more than 20 years of dedicated research and development, Biome Bioplastics has established itself as a leading innovator in high-performance, plant-based bioplastics that are 100% biodegradable and compostable while maintaining compatibility with conventional plastic processing methods.

The company's mission centers on producing bioplastics that can challenge the dominance of oil-based polymers and ultimately replace them completely. With operations from its Starpol Technology Centre facility in Marchwood Industrial Park, Biome Bioplastics develops and produces bioplastics specifically designed to overcome limitations of earlier-generation bioplastic materials.

### Primary Technologies and Materials

**Starch-Based Bioplastics**: Biome Bioplastics produces high-performance plant-based bioplastics derived from renewable feedstocks including starch and cellulose. These materials offer superior mechanical properties compared to simple starch formulations.

**Advanced Polymer Development**: The company develops biodegradable polymers with enhanced properties including high-temperature performance, superior flexibility, and improved processability through standard manufacturing equipment (injection molding, film extrusion, thermoforming, lamination).

**Lignin-Based Innovations**: Commercial bio-based materials are now being produced using chemicals extracted from lignin, representing next-generation bioplastic chemistry from renewable wood-based feedstocks.

**Custom Formulations**: Biome Bioplastics creates customized polymer blends tailored to specific application requirements, enabling manufacturers to optimize material properties for their specific processes and end-use applications.
