## Overview

Berry Global Group, Inc. was a major global manufacturer and marketer of innovative plastic packaging and engineered products headquartered in Evansville, Indiana. Operating over 250 locations worldwide with 40,000+ employees, the company specialized in innovative packaging solutions with strong emphasis on sustainability and circular economy principles.

**Important Note**: Berry Global was acquired by Amcor on April 30, 2025, and now operates as part of Amcor's integrated global packaging business.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Plastic Packaging Portfolio:**
- Rigid plastic containers and bottles
- Flexible films and pouches
- Injection-molded parts and closures
- Specialized packaging components
- Engineered products for diverse applications

**Bio-Based & Sustainable Materials:**
- **Bio-PE (Biobased Polyethylene)**: Drop-in replacement polyethylene from renewable sources
- **Bio-PP (Biobased Polypropylene)**: Bio-based polypropylene for packaging applications
- **Compostable Plastics**: Certified to EN 13432 international standards
- **Recycled Content Products**: Integration of post-consumer and post-industrial recycled materials
- **Specialty Sustainable Solutions**: Advanced bio-derived and circular-focused packaging

**Specialized Product Lines:**
- Medical and healthcare packaging
- Food service containers and wipes
- Consumer product packaging
- Agricultural packaging solutions

### Market Segments

Berry Global served diverse industries with specialized packaging solutions:
