## Overview

Sealed Air Corporation is a major American protective packaging and food packaging company headquartered in Charlotte, North Carolina. Founded in 1957, the company is globally recognized for innovation in packaging systems, protective materials, and food safety solutions. With approximately 25,000 employees serving customers in 175 countries, Sealed Air is a leading provider of packaging solutions addressing product protection, food safety, and sustainability challenges across diverse industries.

The company specializes in protective packaging, food preservation, hygiene solutions, and specialized packaging systems designed to extend product shelf life, reduce waste, and support customer sustainability goals.
