## Overview

Dow Inc. is a major global chemical and materials company headquartered in Midland, Michigan. Founded in 1897 by chemist Herbert Henry Dow, the company has evolved into a diversified chemical and plastics producer with approximately 35,900 employees across manufacturing sites in 31 countries. Dow is a market leader in polyolefins, polyurethane precursors, specialty polymers, and chemicals serving diverse industrial sectors globally.

The company operates through multiple business segments focused on core chemistry, plastics, and advanced materials. Dow maintains commitment to operational excellence, innovation, and increasingly, sustainable material solutions addressing evolving customer and market demands.
