## Overview

Lenzing AG is Austria's world-leading cellulose fiber producer with over 80 years of experience in developing sustainable fibers from renewable resources. The company specializes in innovative fiber technologies including TENCEL® Lyocell and Modal, VEOCEL™, and LENZING™ ECOVERO™. Lenzing operates a sophisticated biorefinery utilizing 100% of wood components with closed-loop production processes. The company has strengthened its position by acquiring a controlling stake in Swedish innovation company TreeToTextile AB for next-generation cellulose fiber development.

## Sustainable Production Technology

**Closed-Loop Biorefinery Process**:
- Uses 100% of wood components
- Recovers more than 99.8% of production solvents
- Feeds recovered solvents back into production loop
- Results in close-to-zero wastage

**Environmental Performance**:
- TENCEL® Lyocell and Modal: 50% less carbon emissions compared to generic alternatives
- TENCEL® Lyocell and Modal: 50% less water consumption
- Biodegradable in soil, freshwater, and marine environments (TÜV AUSTRIA certified)
- Compostable under home and industrial conditions

## Sustainability Certifications

## Strategic Expansion: TreeToTextile Investment

In 2025, Lenzing acquired a controlling stake in TreeToTextile AB, a Swedish innovation company developing next-generation cellulose fiber with:

- **Technology**: Pure cellulose fiber from sustainably managed forests
- **Performance**: 70-90% emission reduction vs. cotton, viscose, and polyester
- **Facilities**: Demonstration plant in Nymölla, Sweden; planned industrial-scale facility
- **Goal**: Revolutionize textile industry through advanced fiber technology

This investment positions Lenzing as a leader in emerging cellulose fiber technologies while maintaining innovation in established products.
