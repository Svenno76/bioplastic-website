## Overview

Blue Ocean Closures (BOC) is a Swedish sustainability-focused technology startup founded in 2021, headquartered in Karlstad with a production facility in Säffle. The company specializes in developing and manufacturing innovative fiber-based closures and caps that eliminate plastic from packaging while maintaining functionality and performance.

With a clear mission to reduce global plastic waste and carbon footprints, Blue Ocean Closures offers an alternative to traditional plastic caps and closures using sustainably sourced cellulose fibers from forest-certified sources.

### Technology & Materials

**Proprietary Sintering Technology:** Blue Ocean Closures uses a patented vacuum press forming technology to sinter cellulose fibers together in a dry format. This innovation creates:
- Plastic-free closure solutions
- Paper-recyclable products (standard composting streams)
- Ocean-biodegradable materials
- Performance equivalent to plastic alternatives

**Feedstock:** Cellulose fibers sourced from sustainably managed forests (FSC-certified sources), the same material composition found in paper products.

### Product Portfolio

**Standard 38mm Fiber Caps:** Designed to replace plastic closures in supplement jars, nutrition products, and personal care containers. Suitable for dry content applications including powders, nutraceuticals, and food ingredients.

**Custom Fiber Closures:** Tailored solutions for specific packaging applications, including bottle caps for alcoholic beverages and other consumer products.

### Market Applications

- **Food & Beverage:** Supplement jars, nutraceutical containers, dry food packaging, spirits bottle closures
- **Personal Care:** Powder-based cosmetics and health products
- **Industrial Packaging:** Dry content containers in various sectors

### Strategic Partnerships

- **The Absolut Company:** Collaborated to develop FSC fiber-based closures with integrated seal barriers for Absolut Vodka bottles, combining sustainability with product performance
- **Great Earth:** Full commercial rollout of fiber-based screw caps for supplement jars, representing one of the first large-scale implementations of molded fiber closures in the health and wellness sector

### Sustainability Impact

Blue Ocean Closures' solutions deliver:
- Complete plastic elimination from closures
- Significant carbon footprint reduction compared to plastic alternatives
- Recyclability as standard paper waste
- Ocean biodegradability for products lost in marine environments
