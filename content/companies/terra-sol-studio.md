## Overview

Terra Sol Studio is an Australian design and product development company focused on creating innovative, sustainable solutions using advanced bioplastic materials. The studio bridges the gap between biotechnology innovation and consumer market applications, specializing in transforming cutting-edge biopolymers into practical, commercially viable products.

Working in partnership with EcoPHA Biotech, Terra Sol Studio brings design expertise and market commercialization capabilities to develop a range of consumer and industrial products made from PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoate) bioplastics. Their design philosophy emphasizes both environmental sustainability and functional performance.

### Core Competencies

- **Sustainable Product Design**: Development of eco-friendly consumer products
- **Bioplastic Application Development**: Translating biopolymers into real-world products
- **Design for Circularity**: Creating products with end-of-life biodegradation in mind
- **Commercial Viability**: Balancing sustainability with market requirements and cost-effectiveness
- **Prototype to Production**: Supporting the transition from concept to manufacturing

### Product Portfolio

In collaboration with EcoPHA Biotech, Terra Sol Studio has developed:
- Compostable food service items (cups, lids, straws, cutlery)
- Sustainable packaging solutions
- Industrial applications of PHA materials

### Strategic Vision

Terra Sol Studio is committed to advancing the circular economy by creating products that naturally decompose without environmental harm. The studio's work demonstrates that sustainable materials can be both functionally superior and commercially viable.

### Strategic Partnerships

- **EcoPHA Biotech**: Technology and material supplier for PHA-based product development
