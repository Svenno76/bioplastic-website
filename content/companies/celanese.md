## Overview

Celanese Corporation is a leading global chemistry and specialty materials company headquartered in Dallas, Texas, USA. With net sales of $9.7 billion, approximately 13,300 employees, and 50 owned and operated manufacturing locations in 27 countries, Celanese is a major player in the bioplastics industry. The company's BioPolymer Solutions represents a family of bio-based and biodegradable materials engineered as alternatives to traditional petroleum-based plastics.

Celanese actively contributes to the development of sustainable packaging solutions through its innovative cellulosic materials that are both bio-based and biodegradable.

### Primary Materials

**Clarifoil Cellulosic Film**: Celanese has pioneered and refined this line of bio-based, biodegradable, industrial and home compostable cellulose diacetate films for over 70 years. Clarifoil cellulosic film offers excellent clarity, printability, and barrier properties while being fully compostable in both industrial and home composting environments.

**BlueRidge Cellulosic Pellets**: Launched in October 2020, BlueRidge cellulosic pellets bring sustainability benefits and performance attributes to rigid plastic containers. These pellets serve as a home compostable alternative to single-use plastics, enabling the production of sustainable rigid packaging solutions.

**Bio-based Content**: Celanese's cellulosic materials are derived from renewable wood pulp sources, providing a truly bio-based alternative to fossil-based plastics with the added benefit of biodegradability and compostability.

### Market Segments

Celanese's BioPolymer Solutions serve diverse market segments:
- **Flexible Packaging**: Films for food packaging, pouches, and wraps
- **Rigid Containers**: Sustainable alternatives for single-use plastic containers
- **Food Packaging**: Compostable packaging for fresh produce and food products
- **Home Compostable Products**: Consumer-friendly compostable solutions
- **Industrial Packaging**: Large-scale packaging applications
- **Specialty Applications**: Custom cellulosic solutions for specific needs
