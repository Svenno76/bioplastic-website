## Overview

Isiah Enterprises (formerly One World Products, Inc.) is an OTC-listed bioplastics and sustainable materials company headquartered in Midland, Michigan, under the leadership of NBA legend and entrepreneur Isiah Thomas. The company acquired Eco Bio Plastics, establishing itself as a U.S.-based manufacturer specializing in renewable bioplastics, compostable materials, and advanced biocomposites from plant-based and recycled feedstocks.

Isiah Enterprises represents an innovative convergence of sports entrepreneurship and advanced materials development, with strategic focus on automotive and packaging applications. The company leverages proprietary processing and compounding technology to convert biomass, recycled materials, and other renewable feedstocks into high-performance bioplastics and composite materials suitable for demanding industrial applications.
