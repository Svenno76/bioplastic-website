## Overview

**⚠️ CLARIFICATION:** Poonawalla Group is a diversified Indian conglomerate, **not a bioplastics manufacturer**. This entry exists to document the Group's strategic investment in the bioplastics sector via **Regeno**, a biodegradable packaging startup.

## Poonawalla Group — Core Businesses

### Serum Institute of India (SII)
- **World's largest vaccine manufacturer** by volume
- **COVID-19**: Produced Covishield (Oxford-AstraZeneca) — billions of doses
- **Portfolio**: 20+ vaccines, 1.5+ billion doses annually
- **Revenue**: $2+ billion; **Key driver** of Group wealth

### Poonawalla Fincorp
- **NBFC** (Non-Banking Financial Company), listed on BSE/NSE
- **Focus**: Consumer finance, MSME lending, vehicle finance
- **AUM**: ₹20,000+ crore

### Poonawalla Stud Farms
- **Premier thoroughbred breeding** operation in India
- **Champions**: Multiple Indian Derby winners, international success

### Poonawalla Engineering / El-O-Matic / Intervalve
- **Industrial valves**, actuators, flow control solutions
- **Oil & gas, power, chemical** sectors

## Strategic Investment: Regeno (2024-2025)

### The Investment
- **Investors**: Yohan Poonawalla & Michelle Poonawalla (scions of the family)
- **Target**: **Regeno** — next-gen material science startup
- **Announced**: February 2025 (BusinessToday, ET Edge, Times Now, StartupTalky)
- **Stake**: Undisclosed; described as "significant entry into sustainability space"

### Regeno — Portfolio Company
- **Technology**: 100% biodegradable, water-soluble, non-toxic packaging films
- **Feedstock**: Plant-based polymers (details proprietary)
- **Differentiation**: Dissolves in water, leaves no microplastics, home-compostable
- **Applications**: Detergent pods, agro-chemical packaging, food wraps, medical
- **Stage**: Pre-commercial / pilot scale

## Why This Matters for Bioplastics

### Signal Value
- **Deep-pocketed industrial family** entering biomaterials
- **Pharma-grade quality mindset** applied to packaging
- **Patient capital** for long R&D cycles
- **Distribution leverage**: SII's global reach could aid Regeno scaling

### Strategic Logic
1. **ESG alignment**: Vaccines save lives; sustainable packaging saves planet
2. **Diversification**: Beyond pharma/finance into climate tech
3. **Policy tailwinds**: India's single-use plastic ban (2022), EPR rules
4. **Talent pool**: Pune biotech ecosystem (SII, NCL, IISER, startups)

## What Poonawalla Group Is NOT

- ❌ **Bioplastics producer** — no manufacturing, no pilot plant
- ❌ **Technology developer** — Regeno is independent
- ❌ **Resin supplier** — no polymer production capacity
- ❌ **Converter** — no film extrusion, bag making
