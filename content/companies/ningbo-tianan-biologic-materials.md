## Overview

Ningbo Tianan Biologic Materials Co., Ltd. holds a distinctive position as the world's largest producer of PHBV (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-valerate) biopolymers, with established commercial operations and global market presence spanning over two decades. Founded in 2000 in Ningbo—a major coastal manufacturing hub in Zhejiang Province—Tianan Biologic pioneered proprietary water-based extraction technology for PHA production, achieving a historic first in 2004 as the initial company globally to accomplish commercial PHBV production using this environmentally-advantaged technology.

Operating a dedicated production facility at Beilun Port in Ningbo, Tianan Biologic manufactures 2,000 metric tons annually of PHBV biopolymers under the Enmat brand, serving global markets across packaging, agriculture, disposables, and specialty applications. The company's products are certified for both industrial composting (EN 13432) and marine biodegradation (OK Biodegradable Marine), positioning Tianan Biologic as a pioneer in addressing ocean plastic pollution and advancing circular economy principles.

Tianan Biologic's two-decade operational history, proprietary water-based PHA extraction technology, global certifications, and established customer relationships distinguish it as a proven, reliable manufacturer in the emerging global bioplastics supply chain. The company's consistent production, technical expertise, and commitment to environmental sustainability have established it as an essential player in the global transition toward biodegradable alternatives to conventional plastics.

## Primary Materials and Product Portfolio

### PHBV Biopolymer Composition and Properties

**Material Structure:**
Tianan Biologic's primary product is PHBV—a copolymer of polyhydroxybutyrate (PHB) and polyhydroxyvalerate (PHV) components:

**Copolymer Composition:**
- **Polyhydroxybutyrate (PHB):** Primary structural component
- **Polyhydroxyvalerate (PHV):** Comonomeric component enhancing flexibility
- **Ratio Tuning:** Variable PHB/PHV ratios enabling diverse properties
- **Bacterial Origin:** Fermentation-derived from microbial processes
- **100% Bio-Based:** Renewable feedstock sourcing

**Material Properties:**
- Thermoplastic with processability similar to conventional plastics
- Tunable crystallinity through composition adjustment
- Mechanical properties from rigid to flexible depending on formulation
- Biodegradability in soil, compost, and marine environments
- No toxic residues upon degradation

### Enmat Brand Product Lines

**Trademark Branding:**
Tianan Biologic markets its PHBV products under the established Enmat trademark, recognized globally as a premium biodegradable polymer:

**Product Grades and Formulations:**
- **Standard PHBV:** General-purpose biodegradable polymer
- **High-Flexibility Grades:** Enhanced elongation for stretchy applications
- **High-Stiffness Grades:** Rigid formulations for structural applications
- **Specialty Formulations:** Custom compositions for specific applications
- **Food-Contact Grades:** Compliance for food packaging applications
