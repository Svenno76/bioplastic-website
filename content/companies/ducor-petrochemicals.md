## Overview

Ducor Petrochemicals is a Netherlands-based polypropylene (PP) producer headquartered in Rozenburg, specializing in sustainable variants of polypropylene for diverse industrial applications. The company distinguishes itself by offering bio-based, bio-circular, and recycled polypropylene variants, enabling customers to transition to more sustainable feedstocks without compromising product performance.

With production capacity exceeding 180,000 metric tons annually, Ducor serves automotive, healthcare, packaging, textiles, and consumer goods sectors with high-quality, non-toxic, and recyclable polypropylene solutions.

### Core Business: Sustainable Polypropylene

**Product Range:**
- **Bio-Based Polypropylene:** Made from renewable plant-derived feedstocks
- **Bio-Circular Polypropylene:** Produced from bio-based feedstocks through circular chemistry processes
- **Recycled Polypropylene:** Closed-loop recycled polymers from post-consumer and industrial waste
- **Conventional Polypropylene:** Traditional fossil-based PP for baseline comparisons

**Certification & Standards:**
- **ISCC PLUS Certified:** Mass balance certified sustainable polypropylene
- **ISO 13485:** Quality management system for healthcare and life sciences applications
- **Non-toxic & Recyclable:** Verified for safety and end-of-life recyclability

### Strategic Partnerships

**Blue Circle Olefins Partnership (2025):**
- Joint agreement with Blue Circle Olefins to produce and co-market segregated bio-polypropylene and carbon-recycled polypropylene
- Combines Ducor's production expertise with Blue Circle's base chemical capabilities
- Creates fully circular supply chain for sustainable PP
