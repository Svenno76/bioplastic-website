## Overview

Orinko Advanced Plastics Co., Ltd. is a national high-tech enterprise headquartered in Hefei, Anhui Province, China. Founded in 2008 and publicly listed in 2021, Orinko is a leading developer and manufacturer of high-performance modified polymer materials with over 15 years of expertise in bioplastics innovation. The company specializes in research, development, production, and sales of modified biodegradable materials, particularly polylactic acid (PLA) and related biopolymers.

With 1,800+ employees and four production and R&D centers across China (Hefei, Guangdong, Chongqing, Anqing), Orinko maintains 500,000+ tons total production capacity and has created more than 300 polymer modified materials through its 350+ person R&D team.
