## Overview

Total Corbion PLA is a strategic 50/50 joint venture between TotalEnergies and Corbion, established in 2017 to develop and commercialize PLA biopolymers at large scale. The company was rebranded as TotalEnergies Corbion in 2023 following TotalEnergies' corporate restructuring. Headquartered in Amsterdam, Netherlands, the joint venture operates one of the world's largest commercial PLA production facilities in Rayong, Thailand, with 75,000 MT/year capacity operational since December 2018.

The company is developing an ambitious expansion program with a planned 100,000 MT/year facility in Grandpuits, France, which will establish TotalEnergies Corbion as one of the world's largest integrated PLA producers with combined capacity of 175,000 MT/year. The company's focus on 100% biobased Luminy PLA derived from renewable sugarcane provides significant environmental benefits with 85% CO2 reduction compared to conventional plastics.

## Luminy PLA Technology Platform

### Luminy Brand Portfolio

TotalEnergies Corbion produces comprehensive PLA grades under the **Luminy brand**, representing:

**Luminy Standard Grades**:
- Injection molding resins for rigid packaging and consumer goods
- Film extrusion grades for flexible packaging applications
- Fiber-grade PLA for textile and nonwoven applications
- Specialized performance formulations for demanding applications

**Luminy Recycled Content Grades**:
- Recycled PLA (rPLA) incorporating post-consumer PLA waste
- Closed-loop recycling initiatives supporting circular economy
- Performance equivalent to virgin PLA for most applications
- Sustainability credentials meeting major brand requirements

**Luminy Food Contact Grades**:
- FDA compliant formulations for food contact applications
- EU Commission Declaration of Compliance certifications
- High transparency options for beverage and consumer goods
- Thermal resistance grades for hot-fill applications

### Production Technology and Process

**100% Biobased Feedstock**:
- Sugarcane as exclusive renewable feedstock source
- Direct supply relationships with sugarcane producers
- Integration with existing sugarcane processing infrastructure
- Fermentation of sugarcane sugars to lactic acid
- Advanced polymerization technology converting lactic acid to PLA

**Sustainability Profile**:
- 85% lifecycle CO2 reduction vs. conventional petroleum plastics
- Life cycle assessment (LCA) verified by independent third parties
- Biobased content certification exceeding 99%
- Reduced fossil fuel dependency and petroleum consumption
