## Overview

Balrampur Chini Mills Limited (BCML) is India's leading sugar producer and a publicly listed company on the National Stock Exchange of India (NSE:BCML) with over 90 years of industrial heritage. The company is transitioning into the bioplastics sector through development of India's first integrated sugar-to-PLA (polylactic acid) manufacturing facility in Kumbhi, Uttar Pradesh. Under the brand Balrampur Bioyug, the company will produce 100% biobase, industrially compostable PLA from sugarcane-derived feedstock at a dedicated facility with 75,000 MT/year production capacity.

The Balrampur PLA facility represents a transformational investment of Rs 2,850 crores (~USD $340 million) and demonstrates the convergence of India's abundant sugarcane resources with advanced biopolymer technology. The facility is designed to be fully renewable energy-powered, established at a single integrated site combining sugarcane processing with PLA productionâ€”a unique manufacturing model achieving superior logistics efficiency and supply chain integration.

### Production Facility and Manufacturing

**Kumbhi Facility Specifications**:

- **Scale**: Industrial-scale production for global export
- **Technology**: Modern PLA synthesis and processing
- **Quality Control**: Food-grade manufacturing standards
- **Sustainability Features**: Renewable energy, waste minimization, circular processes

**Manufacturing Process**:
1. **Sugarcane Processing**: Fermentation of sugarcane juice to lactic acid
2. **Lactic Acid Synthesis**: Conversion of sugars to monomer feedstock
3. **Polymerization**: Chemical synthesis to create PLA polymer
4. **Processing**: Pelletization and granulation for customer use
5. **Quality Assurance**: Testing and certification for food contact and composability

**Renewable Energy Integration**:
- Bagasse (sugarcane residue) combustion for process heat
- Biogas generation from fermentation byproducts
- Solar power potential for facility operations
- Minimize external energy sourcing
- Carbon footprint optimization
