## Overview

RWDC Industries is a leading advanced bioplastics manufacturer specializing in polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA) biopolymers marketed under the Solon® brand. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Singapore with operational facilities in Athens, Georgia, RWDC has established itself as a premier innovator in marine-degradable and environmentally-responsive bioplastics. The company's proprietary medium-chain-length PHA technology enables production of fully biodegradable polymers from renewable feedstocks with exceptional performance characteristics, including unprecedented degradation in marine and aquatic environments—a critical capability for addressing ocean plastic pollution.

With $133 million in Series B funding completed in 2020, RWDC has demonstrated investor confidence in its technology and market opportunity. The company is positioned at the intersection of sustainability demand, regulatory pressure, and corporate commitments to address single-use plastic and marine plastic pollution.

### Proprietary Solon® PHA Technology

**Medium-Chain-Length PHA (mcl-PHA)**:
- Proprietary bacterial fermentation process
- Production from renewable feedstocks (plant oils, sugars)
- Superior mechanical properties vs traditional PHA
- Flexibility and processability advantages
- Thermal stability and processing characteristics
- Patent-protected technology platform

**Solon® Brand Characteristics**:
- Fully biodegradable in multiple environments
- Marine degradation capability (6-12 months in ocean environments)
- Soil biodegradation (complete in agricultural settings)
- Water degradation (freshwater and aquatic environments)
- Compostable in industrial composting facilities
- Home composting capable formulations
- Food contact approved grades

**Technical Advantages**:
- Superior elongation-at-break vs conventional PHA
- Improved processing window
- Better thermal stability
- Reduced brittleness compared to other PHAs
- Consistent material properties across formulations
- Performance matching conventional plastics in many applications
