## Overview

Benvic is a global expert for tailor-made thermoplastic compounds and alternative solutions, headquartered in Chevigny-Saint-Sauveur, France. Founded in 1963, Benvic has established itself as a leading PVC compounder with manufacturing plants across Europe. The company develops, produces, and markets PVC-based thermoplastic solutions in powder and compound form for diverse industrial applications.

In recent years, Benvic has significantly expanded its portfolio beyond traditional PVC to include bioplastic and bio-based alternatives, positioning itself as a compounder of sustainable materials for eco-designed products.

### Core Business

Benvic specializes in thermoplastic compounds across multiple polymer families:
- **PVC Compounds:** Traditional polyvinyl chloride-based solutions
- **Bioplastic Compounds:** PLA (polylactic acid) and PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates) formulations
- **Polyolefin Compounds:** PE and PP-based alternatives
- **TPE Compounds:** Thermoplastic elastomer solutions

### Plantura Brand

Benvic's bioplastic and bio-based product lines operate under the **Plantura brand**, comprising a range of compostable and bio-based polymers designed for eco-conscious product development. Plantura materials target high-end applications including medical devices, healthcare products, designer furniture, and agricultural solutions.

### Market Focus

The company serves multiple industrial sectors:
- Building and construction materials
- Automotive and aerospace components
- Electrical cabling systems
- Pharmaceutical and healthcare applications
- Food and beverage packaging
- Fluid transport systems

### Recent Developments

In August 2022, Benvic was acquired by International Chemical Investors Group, strengthening its position in the bioplastics market and enabling expanded investment in sustainable compound development.
