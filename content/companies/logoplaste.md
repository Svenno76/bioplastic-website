## Overview

Logoplaste is an international reference in rigid plastic packaging, operating through a unique business model that integrates packaging production directly into their customers' production facilities. This "Hole in the Wall" approach eliminates the need for transport of empty bottles, reducing carbon emissions and packaging waste.

The company has been at the forefront of sustainable packaging innovation, investing in lightweighting, recycled content incorporation, and design for recyclability. With manufacturing plants across Europe, North America, South America, and Asia, Logoplaste serves leading global brands in food, beverages, personal care, and healthcare.

### Sustainability Commitments

Logoplaste focuses on reducing the environmental impact of its packaging through material efficiency, recycled content usage, and collaboration with brand owners to meet circular economy targets.
