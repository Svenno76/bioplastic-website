## Overview

Zerocircle is an innovative Indian material science company founded in 2020, pioneering proprietary seaweed-based biopolymer technology as a sustainable alternative to petroleum-derived plastics. Based in India and operating across the Asia-Pacific region, Zerocircle has developed a unique approach to converting sustainably harvested seaweed into 100% natural, fully biodegradable films, coatings, and specialty paper products that provide genuine environmental benefits without synthetic additives or fossil components.

The company's distinctive positioning—combining complete naturalness, rapid home compostability, ocean safety, and strong sustainability credentials—appeals to environmentally conscious brands and consumers across food, fashion, pharmaceutical, and consumer goods sectors. Zerocircle represents the emerging wave of seaweed-based innovation originating from Asia, addressing growing regional demand for sustainable packaging solutions.
