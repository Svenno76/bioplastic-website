## Overview

Sway is a California-based biomaterials company specializing in seaweed-based bioplastics for sustainable single-use products and packaging applications. Founded in the 2010s, the company has developed proprietary technology to convert seaweed polymers into compostable straws, cups, utensils, and flexible packaging materials that match conventional plastic performance while delivering genuine environmental benefits.

Sway's distinctive approach combines seaweed-based material science with regenerative coastal sourcing practices, partnering with seaweed farmers and coastal communities in Latin America to build sustainable, economically beneficial supply chains while supporting ocean health and ecosystem regeneration.

### Seaweed-Based Material Technologies

**Seaweed-Derived Biopolymers**:
- Natural polymers extracted from cultivated seaweed
- Alternative to PLA and PHA conventional production
- Performance properties matching conventional plastics

- Fully compostable formulations

**Key Material Characteristics**:
- 100% renewable marine-derived feedstocks
