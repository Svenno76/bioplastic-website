## Overview

Huhtamaki Oyj is a major global packaging company headquartered in Espoo, Finland. Founded in 1920, the company has evolved into a leading provider of food and beverage packaging solutions operating across more than 30 countries with approximately 18,000 employees. Huhtamaki specializes in designing and producing innovative, sustainable packaging solutions for food service, ready meals, and consumer goods industries globally.

The company is uniquely positioned in the sustainable packaging market, emphasizing molded fiber technology, compostable materials, and eco-friendly solutions that address environmental challenges while meeting stringent food safety and performance requirements.
