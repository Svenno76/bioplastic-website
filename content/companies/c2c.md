## Overview

C2C is a Polish biodegradable packaging manufacturer based in Lublin, Poland, specializing in the production of fully compostable films, bags, and packaging solutions made from corn starch and natural composite materials. Founded in 2008 as a family business, C2C has grown to 50-100 employees and operates modern manufacturing facilities producing innovative packaging solutions combining sustainability with practical performance requirements.

The company's strategic focus on corn starch-based materials and natural composites represents a distinctive approach to bioplastic packaging, emphasizing renewable, food-crop derived materials rather than petroleum-based alternatives. C2C's innovation in bacteriostatic layers, oxobiodegradable formulations, and composting-facility compatibility demonstrates the company's commitment to solving real-world packaging challenges while advancing environmental sustainability objectives.

## Company History and Development

**Founding and Early Growth (2008-2012)**
C2C was established in February 2008 as a family-owned business initially focused on producing plastic films and bags for food packaging and industrial applications. The company was based in Zawieprzycka, Lublin, in the Lubelskie region of eastern Poland, positioning itself to serve Central and Eastern European markets.

**Biodegradable Transition (2012-2016)**
Beginning around 2012-2013, C2C made the strategic decision to develop biodegradable and compostable packaging alternatives. This transition reflected:
- Recognition of growing regulatory pressure on single-use plastics
- Customer demand for sustainable packaging solutions
- Opportunity to differentiate from conventional plastic producers
- Environmental commitment of company leadership

**Certification and Innovation (2016-2020)**
The company achieved significant milestones:
- **2016**: First Polish company to receive OK Compost Home certification from TÜV Austria for bio-compostable film
- Development of proprietary bacteriostatic formulations for food packaging
- Integration of oxobiodegradable layers for accelerated decomposition
- TÜV certification validating product claims and performance

**Market Expansion and Recognition (2020-Present)**
C2C expanded market presence through:
- Growth to 50-100 employees
- Establishment as recognized innovation leader in Polish packaging
- Recognition through innovation and sustainability awards
- Geographic expansion across Central European markets
