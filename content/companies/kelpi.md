## Overview

Kelpi is a British biotechnology company headquartered in Bath, United Kingdom, founded in 2020 by Neil Morris. The company is pioneering a new generation of bioplastics made entirely from seaweed, designed to replace single-use fossil fuel plastics while delivering superior performance and environmental benefits.

With Series A funding of $9.51M from 17 investors, Kelpi is scaling production of seaweed-based bioplastics that are compostable, marine-safe, and biodegradable while maintaining world-leading water barrier properties.

### Core Technology: Seaweed-Based Bioplastics

**Seaweed Feedstock:**
- Rapidly renewable resource (no fertilizers, land, or fresh water required)
- Grows prolifically in coastal regions
- Absorbs CO₂ as it grows
- De-acidifies and re-oxygenates ocean environments
- Zero impact on food security or agricultural land use

**Material Properties:**
- Compostable and marine-safe bioplastics
- World-leading water barrier performance
- Superior alternative to fossil fuel plastics
- Performance-matched to conventional plastic coatings
- Suitable for high-barrier applications

**Environmental Benefits:**
- Carbon sequestering (algae absorbs CO₂ during growth)
- Ocean health improvement
- No plastic pollution
- Fully biodegradable
- Supports circular ocean economy

### Product Portfolio

**Seaweed-Based Bioplastic Coatings:**
- Food packaging applications
