## Overview

RWDC Industries is a Singapore-based advanced biopolymer company specializing in the development and commercialization of proprietary mcl-PHA (medium-chain-length polyhydroxyalkanoate) technology. Founded in 2015, the company has rapidly established itself as a leading innovator in PHA bioplastics, achieving significant market validation through major funding rounds and strategic partnerships with leading consumer brands.

Operating production facilities in Athens, Georgia (USA), RWDC has established North American manufacturing presence while maintaining Singapore-based research and development and global business operations. The company's proprietary Solon brand PHA materials represent an advanced innovation in bacterial fermentation-derived biopolymers with proven environmental credentials spanning soil, water, and marine biodegradation.

**Medium-Chain-Length PHA Specialization**:
- Proprietary mcl-PHA technology differentiation
- Produced via advanced bacterial fermentation processes
- Plant oils and sugar feedstocks converted to PHA
- Controlled molecular structure enabling diverse properties
- Advanced material properties vs commodity PHA

**Fermentation-Based Production**:

- Optimized fermentation conditions
- Efficient feedstock utilization
- Scalable manufacturing process

- Biodegradable in soil environments
- Biodegradable in freshwater

- Customizable for specific applications
