## Overview

Asahi Kasei Corporation is a multinational Japanese chemical company headquartered in Tokyo, founded in 1922. The company is a diversified manufacturer centered on chemistry, with operations spanning three main business sectors: Material (consisting of fibers, chemicals, and electronics), Homes (consisting of homes and construction materials), and Health care (consisting of pharmaceuticals, medical devices). The company is committed to sustainable bio-based materials and circular economy solutions.

The Asahi Kasei Group employs approximately 46,000 people globally and contributes to life and living around the world through its wide-ranging operations and innovations in bioplastics and sustainable materials.

### Primary Materials

**Revolefin Technology**: Asahi Kasei's proprietary Revolefin technology produces basic chemicals from bioethanol, enabling the production of bio-based plastics. The plastics produced from basic chemicals using this technology are categorized as bio-based plastics, offering a renewable alternative to fossil-based materials.

**Engineering Plastics Portfolio**:
- **Polyacetal (POM) resins**: High-performance engineering thermoplastic
- **Polyamide (PA, nylon) resins**: Durable synthetic polymers for various applications
- **Modified Polyphenylene Ether (PPE) resins**: Heat-resistant engineering plastics

**Bio-based Materials**: The company focuses on producing basic chemicals from bioethanol and converting them into bioplastics, contributing to reduced carbon emissions and sustainable material solutions.

### Market Segments

Asahi Kasei serves diverse market segments with its bioplastics and materials:
- **Automotive**: Engineering plastics for vehicle components
- **Electronics**: High-performance polymers for electronic devices
- **Construction Materials**: Building and construction applications
- **Medical Devices**: Biocompatible materials for healthcare
- **Pharmaceuticals**: Drug delivery systems and pharmaceutical applications
- **Packaging**: Sustainable packaging solutions
- **Consumer Goods**: Various bio-based product applications
