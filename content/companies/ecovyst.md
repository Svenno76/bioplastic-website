## Overview

Ecovyst Inc. is a leading integrated provider of advanced materials, specialty catalysts, and sulfuric acid regeneration services headquartered in Philadelphia. Launched in 2021 as a pure-play catalysts and services company following the sale of its Performance Chemicals business, Ecovyst focuses on high-growth catalyst markets supporting plastics production, sustainable chemistry, and renewable energy transitions.

The company's Advanced Silicas division provides critical catalyst supports and functionalized materials enabling the production of polyethylene, bioplastics, and sustainable chemical syntheses globally.

### Core Business: Catalysts & Advanced Materials

**Product Lines:**
- **Advanced Silicas:** Finished catalyst supports and functionalized materials
- **Zeolite Catalysts:** Molecular sieve catalysts for chemical processing
- **Specialty Catalysts:** AlphaCat chemical catalysis solutions
- **Regeneration Services:** Spent catalyst recycling and regeneration

### Advanced Silicas for Bioplastics

**Role in Bioplastic Production:**
Ecovyst's advanced silicas and functionalized materials serve as essential catalyst supports and process catalysts for:
- **Bioplastic Synthesis:** Enabling catalytic conversion of bio-based feedstocks into biopolymers
- **Polyester Production:** Supporting catalysts for PET and polyester fiber manufacture
- **Sustainable Chemistry:** Catalyzing renewable feedstock conversion to high-value chemicals

**Technology & Applications:**
- Silica-based catalysts for polymer synthesis
- Zeolite-based catalysts for chemical transformations
- Customized catalyst supports for specific process requirements
- High-performance materials for demanding chemical environments
