## Overview

Kemvera is a sustainable chemistry technology company specializing in advanced catalytic platform technologies that transform bio-based and CO₂-derived feedstocks into cost-effective, sustainable chemicals. The company leverages domestically sourced corn ethanol to produce high-performance, drop-in replacements for conventional petroleum-derived chemicals, supporting the circular economy transition while backing American agricultural producers.

### Core Technology: Catalytic Conversion Platform

**Advanced Catalytic Technology:**
- Proprietary platform converts bio-based feedstocks into sustainable chemicals
- CO₂-derived feedstock utilization capabilities
- Drop-in replacement performance equivalent to fossil-derived chemicals
- Cost-competitive with conventional alternatives

**Feedstock Focus:**
- Domestically sourced U.S. corn ethanol (cost-advantaged)
- Renewable and sustainable sourcing
- Support for American corn farming communities
- Circular economy alignment

### Flagship Products

**Bio-Acetic Acid:**
- Bio-based alternative to petroleum acetic acid
- Industrial-scale production capability
- Cost-effective formulation
- Applications: footwear, disinfectants, coatings

**Bio-Ethyl Acetate:**
- Sustainable alternative to conventional ethyl acetate
- Performance-matched to fossil-derived product
- Industrial applications
- Green solvent capabilities

### Production Scale & Milestones

**Development Timeline:**
- 20-metric-tons-per-year pilot reactor: Recently commissioned and validated
- 500-metric-tons-per-year pre-commercial demonstration reactor: Designed and ready for deployment
- 50,000-metric-tons-per-year commercial-scale plant: Process design package completed

**Scale-Up Progress:**
- Continuous operations validated through pilot testing
- Robustness demonstrated across operational conditions
- Ready for commercial plant deployment

### Market Applications

**Target Industries:**
