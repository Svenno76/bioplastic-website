## Overview

Entec Polymers is a leading distributor and converter of thermoplastic resins and advanced polymer solutions, headquartered in Orlando, Florida. Founded in 1987 and now part of the Ravago Group, Entec has spent over 35 years as a specialist in recycled, bio-based, and compostable polymer distribution across the Americas, serving customers in the United States, Canada, Mexico, the Caribbean, and Central and South America.

The company bridges materials manufacturers with converters and manufacturers, enabling the transition to more sustainable, circular biopolymer solutions across diverse industries.

### Core Business: Sustainable Polymer Distribution

**Service Model:**
- Technical sales and application support
- Resin distribution and logistics
- Polymer selection consulting
- Market-specific solutions for diverse end-uses

**Product Portfolio:**
- Recycled thermoplastic resins (post-consumer and post-industrial)
- Bio-based and bioplastic polymers
- Certified compostable materials
- Specialty additives and compounds
- Resin blends optimized for specific applications

### Strategic Partnerships

**BASF Collaboration:**
- Exclusive distribution of BASF ecoflex® (certified compostable plastic)
- Distribution of ecovio® (bio-based copolyester)
- Full product portfolio support from BASF's sustainable plastics range

**Loliware Partnership (2025+):**
- Recent distribution agreement with Loliware Inc.
- Seaweed-based biopolymer solutions
- Expanded portfolio with ocean-derived sustainable materials
- Support for manufacturers seeking innovative bioplastic alternatives

### Product Lines

**Compostable Polymers (ecoflex®):**
- Certified biodegradable in industrial composting
- Applications: Flexible films, bags, packaging, agriculture
- Home compostable and marine biodegradable grades available

**Bio-Based Polymers (ecovio®):**
- Biobased content from renewable resources
- Applications: Packaging, flexible films, injection molding
- Reduced carbon footprint vs. conventional polymers

**Seaweed-Based Materials (Loliware):**
- Ocean-derived raw materials
- High-performance biopolymer applications
- Innovative sustainable alternative to petroleum-based plastics

**Recycled Polymers:**
- Post-consumer recycled (PCR) resins
- Post-industrial recycled (PIR) materials
- Certified sustainable sourcing
