## Overview

ProfiKomp Environmental Technologies Inc. is a leading Hungarian environmental engineering and biowaste treatment company headquartered in Gödöllő, Hungary. Founded in 2000 (with origins tracing to 1991 in early biotechnology work), ProfiKomp has established itself as Europe's foremost specialist in composting infrastructure design, biowaste treatment optimization, and materials testing including bioplastic degradation assessment.

The company operates a global network of over 85 closed, semi-permeable, membrane-covered aerated biowaste treatment systems worldwide, representing decades of practical experience in composting facility design and operation. Beyond infrastructure, ProfiKomp operates a sophisticated research laboratory conducting independent testing of bioplastic materials, compost properties, and waste stream characteristics. The company's integration of practical composting experience with laboratory research capabilities positions ProfiKomp uniquely as both an infrastructure provider and materials testing authority.

ProfiKomp's origins trace to 1991, when founding principles around advanced biowaste treatment were developed through more than a decade of professional experience in environmental engineering and waste management.
