## Overview

Corbion is a leading Dutch biochemical company headquartered in Amsterdam, Netherlands, with a rich history dating back to 1919 (founded as CSM - Centrale Suiker Maatschappij). The company has evolved into a specialized producer of high-purity lactic acid, lactide monomers, and other biochemical solutions that form the foundation for advanced bioplastics and biomaterials production. Corbion is a publicly listed company on Euronext Amsterdam and maintains operations and facilities across Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

As a pioneer in fermentation technology and bioprocess development, Corbion has established itself as an essential supplier to the bioplastics industry. The company's strategic partnership with TotalEnergies through the Total Corbion PLA joint venture represents a landmark collaboration in scaling bioplastics production to industrial volumes.

### Primary Materials and Products

**Lactic Acid**: Corbion's core product, produced through fermentation of renewable feedstocks
- High-purity grade suitable for PLA monomer synthesis
- Bio-based alternative to chemical synthesis routes
- Produced using Corbion's proprietary fermentation technologies
- Feedstock for lactide monomer production

**Lactide Monomers**: Intermediate products essential for PLA polymerization
- Purified lactide used in PLA resin production
- Critical input for Total Corbion PLA operations
- Enables the production of high-quality, food-grade PLA polymers

**Polylactic Acid (PLA)**: Corbion's involvement through Total Corbion PLA joint venture
- Through Total Corbion PLA: Direct production and marketing of PLA resins
- Supply of raw materials (lactic acid and lactide) as independent producer
- Supporting the broader PLA supply chain

**Biochemicals and Biomaterials**: Expanding portfolio of sustainable chemistry products
- Fermentation-derived chemicals for various industries
- Food and feed additives
- Specialty chemicals for industrial applications

### Total Corbion PLA Joint Venture

**Joint Venture Structure**: 50/50 partnership between Corbion and TotalEnergies, established in November 2016.

**Commercial PLA Plant**: A 75,000 tons per year capacity PLA plant in Rayong, Thailand, which commenced operations on December 3, 2018. This facility represents one of the world's largest dedicated PLA production plants.

**Strategic Significance**: The partnership combines:
- Corbion's expertise in lactic acid fermentation and PLA technology
- TotalEnergies' petrochemical scale and global market access
- Advanced production capabilities supporting global PLA demand

**Market Reach**: Total Corbion PLA serves customers worldwide with certified, high-quality PLA resins suitable for packaging, fibers, and engineering applications.

### Market Segments and Applications

Corbion's lactic acid, lactide, and PLA products serve:
- **Packaging**: PLA-based bottles, films, and rigid containers
- **Food & Beverage**: Food-contact compliant packaging materials
- **Textiles and Fibers**: Bioplastic fibers for clothing and industrial textiles
- **Agriculture**: Biodegradable agricultural films and applications
- **Medical Devices**: Biocompatible and biodegradable medical applications
- **Electronics**: Bioplastic components for consumer electronics
- **Home & Personal Care**: Sustainable packaging for personal care products
- **Pharmaceuticals**: Biocompatible materials for pharmaceutical applications

### Production and Global Presence

**Amsterdam Headquarters**: Center for corporate strategy, research, and development.

**European Operations**: Production facilities and operations across multiple European locations.

**Asia-Pacific Region**: Operations including the strategic Rayong facility through Total Corbion PLA.

**Americas**: Distribution and sales operations serving North and South American markets.

### Company History and Heritage

Corbion's origins in 1919 as CSM (Centrale Suiker Maatschappij) reflect its foundational role in fermentation science. Over more than a century, Corbion has evolved from sugar processing into a specialized biochemicals company, becoming indispensable to the modern bioplastics industry. The company's long history of fermentation expertise provides confidence in the scalability and reliability of lactic acid production for PLA synthesis.

### Industry Leadership and Certifications

Corbion maintains certifications and quality standards essential for food-contact applications and pharmaceutical uses. As an active member of industry organizations, Corbion contributes to advancing bioplastics standards, technology development, and market education initiatives. The company's partnership with TotalEnergies demonstrates confidence from major international energy and chemical corporations in Corbion's lactic acid production capabilities and PLA technology.
