## Overview

Eastman Chemical Company is a leading specialty materials and advanced chemicals manufacturer headquartered in Kingsport, Tennessee. Founded in 1920, the company has evolved into a global materials innovator with approximately 14,000 employees operating across multiple continents. Eastman is committed to creating advanced materials that solve critical sustainability challenges while delivering superior performance across diverse industrial and consumer applications.

The company specializes in developing sustainable alternatives to conventional materials, including cellulosic biopolymers and bio-derived polymers designed to reduce environmental impact while maintaining or exceeding the performance characteristics of traditional petroleum-based materials.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Cellulosic Biopolymers:**
- **Treva®**: Eastman's brand of cellulosic biopolymers offering sustainable alternatives to conventional plastics
- Bio-derived polymers from renewable cellulose sources
- Advanced fiber-forming materials
- Specialty film and coating materials

**Advanced Material Portfolio:**
- High-performance polymers for engineering applications
- Specialty plastics for medical and healthcare
- Advanced adhesives and resins
- Coating and film materials
- Chemical intermediates

**Bio-Based and Sustainable Solutions:**
- Plant-derived polymer precursors
- Bio-based specialty chemicals
- Circular economy-focused materials
- Recycled content integration solutions

### Market Segments

Eastman serves diverse global industries with advanced material solutions:
- **Packaging**: Sustainable films, containers, and protective materials
- **Textiles & Apparel**: Fiber materials, yarns, and textile applications
- **Automotive**: Lightweight components, interior materials, adhesives
- **Construction & Building**: Durable materials for structural and functional applications
- **Personal Care**: Specialty materials for cosmetics and personal care products

- **Industrial Applications**: Advanced chemicals and materials for manufacturing processes

- Reduced carbon footprint compared to conventional plastics
