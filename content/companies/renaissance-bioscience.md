## Overview

Renaissance BioScience is a Canadian biotechnology company specializing in bioengineering yeast and microorganisms for sustainable solutions across agriculture, food, environmental, and energy industries. The company is a global leader in bioengineered yeast development and has partnered with UK-based Biome Bioplastics to develop next-generation renewable bioplastic building blocks through advanced fermentation processes.

## Core Technology and Expertise

- **Yeast Bioengineering**: Advanced genetic modification of yeast strains for specific fermentation outcomes
- **Microbial Fermentation**: Proprietary fermentation processes for bioplastic precursor production
- **Strain Engineering**: Development of high-efficiency microorganism strains
- **Scalable Bioprocessing**: Technology capable of industrial-scale fermentation

## Strategic Partnership: Biome Bioplastics

Renaissance BioScience and Biome Bioplastics have partnered on a two-year, C$1.5 million initiative to:

- **Develop renewable bioplastic building blocks** using yeast and bacterial fermentation
- **Conduct strain engineering and fermentation trials** in Canada and UK
- **Optimize production processes** for scalability
- **Generate test samples** for packaging and personal-care applications with global partners

## Research Focus

The partnership focuses on:
- Producing cost-effective, scalable pathways for renewable plastics
- Reducing fossil-fuel dependence
- Minimizing waste generation
- Meeting performance expectations for everyday products

## Market Applications

- **Packaging**: Food and beverage containers, flexible packaging
- **Personal Care**: Beauty, hygiene, and personal care product packaging
- **Health Products**: Pharmaceutical and healthcare packaging
- **Consumer Goods**: General consumer product applications
