## Overview

Balrampur Chini Mills Limited is an Indian sugar and bioplastics company undertaking a transformative project to establish the country's first industrial-scale PLA (polylactic acid) bioplastics production facility. Based in Kumbhi, Uttar Pradesh, the company is investing Rs 2,850 crores (approximately $340 million USD) in a state-of-the-art facility that will revolutionize India's capacity for sustainable biopolymer production.

The company's innovative Bioyug brand represents a significant leap forward in utilizing India's abundant sugarcane resources for advanced bioplastics production. What distinguishes Balrampur's approach is its vertical integrationâ€”the facility will be the world's first to achieve complete sugar-cane-to-PLA conversion at a single site, eliminating supply chain intermediaries and maximizing economic efficiency.

### Bioyug PLA Technology

**Integrated Production Model**: Unlike traditional PLA producers that source pre-processed feedstocks, Balrampur's facility will handle the entire value chain:
- Sugar extraction from sugarcane
- Fermentation to lactic acid
- Polymerization to PLA resin
- All processes co-located at a single facility in Uttar Pradesh

**PLA Characteristics**:
- 100% plant-based from renewable sugarcane feedstocks
- Biobased and biodegradable polymer
- Industrial and home composting capable
- Compatible with existing processing equipment
- Properties equivalent to conventional plastics
- Significantly reduced carbon footprint

### Production Facility and Capacity

**Scale and Investment**:
- Rs 2,850 crores (~$340 million USD) capital investment
- 75,000 tonnes per year production capacity
- Expected to be operational by October 2026

**Sustainability Features**:

### Industry Position and Significance

**Regional Leadership**: As India's first industrial-scale PLA facility, Balrampur Chini Mills will establish the country as a significant bioplastics producer and reduce India's dependence on imported biopolymers.

**Global Context**: The facility's 75,000 MT/year capacity will position it among the world's major PLA producers, supporting India's transition to sustainable materials and circular economy principles.

**Economic Impact**: The project is expected to create approximately 2,000 direct and indirect jobs in Uttar Pradesh, supporting rural economic development and agricultural communities.

### Strategic Advantages

**Feedstock Security**: Balrampur's established sugar operations provide secure, reliable access to sugarcane feedstock at competitive costs.

**Integrated Operations**: Vertical integration from sugar production to finished PLA resin eliminates supply chain dependencies and improves margins.

**Government Support**: Strong backing from Uttar Pradesh government and central authorities demonstrates policy support for bioplastics development.

**Market Timing**: The facility's October 2026 commissioning aligns with accelerating global demand for sustainable biopolymers and India's packaging regulations favoring biodegradable materials.
