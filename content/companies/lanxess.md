## Overview

LANXESS AG is a major German specialty chemicals company headquartered in Cologne, Germany. Founded in 2004 following a strategic separation of Bayer AG's chemicals division and parts of its polymers business, LANXESS has evolved into a global leader in advanced materials and specialty polymers. The company operates across 32 countries with approximately 11,800 employees, focusing on innovation and sustainability in chemical engineering.

LANXESS specializes in the development, manufacturing, and marketing of chemical intermediates, additives, specialty chemicals, and advanced plastics, with growing emphasis on bio-based materials and sustainable product innovation to meet evolving market demands.

### Primary Materials & Products

**Specialty Polymers & Engineering Plastics:**
- High-performance polyamides (PA) and polyurethanes (PU)
- Polycarbonate blends and compounds
- Engineering thermoplastics for high-temperature applications
- Specialty elastomers and rubber compounds
- Advanced polymer additives and modifiers

**Bio-Based Materials:**
- Bio-derived polyamides from renewable sources
- Bio-based polyurethane systems
- Sustainable polymer compounds
- Green chemistry-based additives and modifiers
- Plant-based raw material integration

**Chemical Intermediates:**
- Specialty chemicals for diverse industrial processes
- Advanced catalysts
- Functional additives for polymer and material enhancement
- Industrial chemical products
