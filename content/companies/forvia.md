## Overview

FORVIA is a global automotive technology group formed in 2022 from the merger of Faurecia and HELLA. The company is a leading provider of sustainable materials and composites for automotive applications, particularly through its MATERI'ACT sustainable materials entity created in November 2022. FORVIA develops innovative biocomposite and bioplastic solutions that significantly reduce vehicle weight and carbon emissions.

## MATERI'ACT Sustainable Materials Division

FORVIA's dedicated sustainable materials entity focuses on:
- Low and ultra-low CO₂ materials from recycled, bio-based, and carbon-capturing compounds
- Development and commercialization of biocomposite solutions
- Integration of recycled and renewable content into automotive components

## Key Product Lines

**NAFILean Biocomposites**:
- 100% recyclable biocomposite materials
- Hemp fiber reinforced polypropylene
- Reduces CO₂ emissions up to 87%
- Already deployed on over 9 million vehicles
- Market presence for over 12 years

**NAFILean Vision**:
- Next-generation biocomposite for injection molding
- Maximizes decarbonization for visible automotive parts
- Enhanced performance and sustainability

**Additional Materials**:
- **Piñatex Advanced**: Pineapple leaf fiber coating, 40% more natural, 98% lower CO₂ emissions
- **Ecorium**: Recycled PET + hemp, 90% CO₂ reduction, 70%+ natural and recycled content

## Industry Focus and Applications

- **Vehicle Interiors**: Dashboards, door panels, trim components
- **Structural Components**: Weight-bearing parts requiring high performance
- **Visible Parts**: Aesthetically important components using biocomposites
- **Circular Design**: Products designed for end-of-life recycling

## Strategic Targets and Commitments

- **2030 Goal**: Integrate 70% recycled plastics into all automotive interior products
- **Scope 3 Objectives**: Address emissions across value chain
- **Circular Economy**: Drive automotive industry transition to sustainable materials

## Geographic Presence

Global operations serving major automotive manufacturers across Europe, North America, and Asia. FORVIA is headquartered in Nanterre, France.
