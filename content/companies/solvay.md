## Overview

Solvay is a major Belgian company in advanced materials and specialty chemicals founded in 1863. Following a demerger at the end of 2023 creating the new Syensqo entity, Solvay has specialized in essential chemistry with 9,000+ employees across 40 countries. The company is headquartered in Brussels, Belgium, with regional operations across Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific.

Solvay is focused on developing essential chemistry solutions supporting diverse industrial sectors, with emphasis on advanced materials, specialty polymers, and chemical innovation addressing global sustainability and performance challenges.
