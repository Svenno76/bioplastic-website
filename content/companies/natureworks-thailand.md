## Overview

NatureWorks Thailand represents a transformative investment in Southeast Asia's bioplastics capacity and positions Thailand as a major global production center for Ingeo polylactic acid (PLA). A joint venture between Cargill, the global agricultural products and services company, and PTT Global Chemical, Thailand's largest petrochemical company, NatureWorks Thailand is constructing a fully-integrated PLA biopolymer facility in Nakhon Sawan Province with an annual production capacity of 75,000 metric tons.

The facility represents a major expansion of NatureWorks' global PLA production capacity and demonstrates the strategic importance of Southeast Asia for advanced biopolymer manufacturing. By sourcing sugarcane from farms within a 50-kilometer radius of the production facility, the project creates a localized renewable feedstock loop and supports agricultural communities while positioning Thailand to serve rapidly growing Asia-Pacific and global bioplastics markets.

With construction on track for 2025 completion and operational commencement, NatureWorks Thailand will become one of the world's largest PLA production facilities and a critical node in global bioplastics supply chains, supporting diverse applications from flexible packaging to hygiene products and food service packaging.

## Polylactic Acid (PLA) Technology and Ingeo Platform

NatureWorks Thailand specializes in Ingeo polylactic acid production, representing one of the world's most commercially successful and widely-adopted biopolymers.

### PLA Chemistry and Ingeo Platform

**Material Composition:**
- Polylactic acid synthesized from lactic acid monomers
- Thermoplastic polyester derived from renewable feedstocks
- Fully biodegradable in industrial composting conditions
- Complete breakdown to biomass, CO₂, and water
- Performance characteristics comparable to conventional polyesters

**Ingeo Brand:**
- NatureWorks' trademarked PLA technology platform
- Advanced lactic acid fermentation and polymerization
- Superior material consistency and quality
- Enhanced performance characteristics vs. commodity PLA
- Proven market acceptance and brand recognition
- Extensive customer base and technical support

**Key Advantages:**
- Established market acceptance and regulatory approvals
- Proven biodegradability in industrial composting systems
- Thermal processing compatibility with conventional equipment
- Food contact compliance across major jurisdictions
- Transparent and naturally clear material suitable for packaging
- Sustainability credentials and environmental benefits

### Integrated Production Process

**Fully-Integrated Production:**
- Integrated facility encompassing entire production chain
- Lactic acid fermentation from sugarcane-derived feedstocks
- Lactide intermediate production and purification
- Advanced polymerization to high-molecular-weight PLA
- Quality assurance and product customization

**Feedstock Integration:**
- Local sugarcane sourcing within 50-kilometer radius
- Direct farmer relationships and supply chain control
- Sustainable cultivation practices and support
- Feedstock freshness and quality assurance
- Vertical integration from agriculture to final product

**Production Stages:**
1. Sugarcane crushing and juice extraction
2. Fermentation to lactic acid
3. Purification and concentration of lactic acid
4. Lactide production through ring-opening polymerization
5. Advanced polymerization to high-molecular-weight PLA
6. Quality testing and product finishing
7. Pelletization and packaging for shipment
