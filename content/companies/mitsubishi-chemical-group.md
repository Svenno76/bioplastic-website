## Overview

Mitsubishi Chemical is a major Japanese chemical company headquartered in Tokyo and founded in 1933, establishing over 90 years of industrial chemistry leadership. Publicly listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange (TSE:4188), the company operates globally with over 1,000 employees and diversified chemical, materials, and bioplastics operations. The company is recognized as one of the world's leading producers of biodegradable polyester copolymers, producing BioPBS and the innovative DURABIO transparent bio-based engineering plastic.

Mitsubishi Chemical's comprehensive bioplastics portfolio addresses global demand for sustainable materials across packaging, textiles, food service, automotive, medical, and consumer goods industries, positioning it as a critical enabler of the circular economy transition.
