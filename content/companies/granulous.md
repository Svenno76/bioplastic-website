## Overview

Granulous is a UK-based bioplastics feedstock supplier specializing in the production and distribution of certified biodegradable plastic pellets and granules for converters and manufacturers. Founded in the 2010s, Granulous has established itself as a reliable, accessible source of eco-friendly plastic materials, enabling companies across diverse industries to transition from conventional plastics to sustainable alternatives without requiring proprietary material development capabilities or specialized equipment investments.

The company occupies a critical niche in the bioplastics value chain—serving as an intermediary between major bioplastic resin producers and converters—providing processing-ready pellets optimized for standard plastics manufacturing equipment. This positioning has made biodegradable materials accessible to small and medium-sized manufacturers who lack the scale or resources for vertical integration while supporting broader market adoption of sustainable polymers.

### Competitive Advantages and Market Positioning

**Processing-Ready Format**:
Granulous provides finished, processing-ready pellets that eliminate the need for manufacturers to invest in compounding equipment, specialized material science expertise, or proprietary formulation development—a significant advantage for smaller manufacturers.

**Accessibility and Market Democratization**:
The company makes biodegradable materials accessible to small and medium-sized manufacturers (SMEs) who lack the scale, expertise, or capital for vertical integration, democratizing access to sustainable materials.

**Material Certification**:
Comprehensive international certifications (EN 13432 for industrial composting, ASTM D6400 for North American composting) provide regulatory validation and customer confidence in environmental claims.

**Equipment Compatibility**:
Formulations are specifically optimized for standard plastics processing machinery (injection molding, extrusion, thermoforming), eliminating the need for converter equipment modifications—critical barrier reduction for adoption.

**Supply Reliability**:
Established supply chains, consistent quality assurance, and reliable material availability provide converters with dependable feedstock.

**Customization Capability**:
Beyond standard formulations, Granulous can develop specialized compounds for specific customer requirements, providing flexibility for emerging applications.

**Cost-Performance Balance**:
Offerings span from cost-optimized commodity grades to performance-enhanced specialty formulations, addressing diverse customer requirements.
