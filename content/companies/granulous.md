## Overview

Granulous is a Finnish startup that produces high-performance, biodegradable bioplastic alternatives made from spent grains, a byproduct of the brewing industry. Their bio-composite material is home compostable, scalable, and designed to replace conventional plastics in short-life applications such as packaging, consumer goods, and agricultural products. Granulous materials are compatible with standard injection molding machinery, facilitating easy adoption.

### Primary Materials

Spent grains-based biocomposite (agricultural waste-derived bioplastic)

### Market Segments

Packaging, consumer goods, agriculture (e.g., plant pots, tree guards, viticulture products), short-life plastic products
