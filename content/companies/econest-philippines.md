## Overview

EcoNest Philippines represents the Philippines' pioneering entry into specialist bioplastics innovation and manufacturing, establishing the country's first dedicated research and development operation focused on regenerative materials from indigenous agricultural feedstocks. Founded in 2018, EcoNest has positioned itself as a catalyst for Philippine bioplastics industry development through innovation in cassava, maize, and bagasse-based biopolymers suitable for food service, agricultural, and specialty consumer applications.

Operating from the Philippines, EcoNest leverages the country's substantial agricultural capacity and emerging manufacturing capabilities to develop biodegradable alternatives to conventional plastics. The company's product portfolio ranges from functional food service items (maize cups and cassava bags) to specialty branded products (plantable cards) and bagasse-based containers, demonstrating the versatility of agricultural waste conversion into high-value bioplastic materials.

EcoNest embodies the Philippines' potential to develop indigenous bioplastics solutions and support the country's circular economy transition while demonstrating that Southeast Asian nations can develop capability in advanced materials manufacturing beyond commodity production.
