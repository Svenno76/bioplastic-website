## Overview

Husky Injection Molding Systems Ltd. is a major Canadian manufacturer of injection molding equipment and systems headquartered in Bolton, Ontario, Canada. With over 1,200 employees at headquarters and global operations, Husky specializes in designing and producing advanced equipment for plastics processing and manufacturing. The company is uniquely positioned as an enabler of bioplastics processing, providing equipment capable of handling both conventional and bio-based polymer materials.

**Important Note**: While Husky manufactures injection molding equipment rather than bioplastics materials directly, the company's equipment is essential infrastructure for bioplastics production and processing, supporting the global bioplastics industry through technological innovation.

### Core Business Focus

**Injection Molding Equipment:**
- PET beverage bottle production systems
- Plastic container and closure manufacturing equipment
- Preform production systems
- Injection-stretch-blow molding (ISBM) technology
- Custom equipment development

**Packaging Solutions:**
- Equipment for food and beverage packaging
- Closure and cap production systems
