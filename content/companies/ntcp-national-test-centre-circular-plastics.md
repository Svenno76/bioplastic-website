## Overview

The National Test Centre Circular Plastics (NTCP) is a not-for-profit independent test and research center focused on accelerating circular plastics recycling. Located in Heerenveen, it features industrial-scale facilities for sorting, washing, shredding, and analyzing heterogeneous plastic waste streams from household sources.
