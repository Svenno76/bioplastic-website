## Overview

LyondellBasell Industries NV is a major global petrochemicals and polymers producer formed in December 2007 through the acquisition of Lyondell Chemical Company by Basell Polyolefins. The company maintains headquarters in Houston, Texas, with corporate offices in London, UK, and operates across multiple continents. With approximately 15,000 employees worldwide, LyondellBasell is a leading producer of polyolefins and specialty polymers serving diverse industrial sectors.

The company is positioned as a key player in the polymer and petrochemical industry, with operations spanning base chemicals, polyolefins, advanced polymers, and specialty materials. While historically petrochemical-focused, the company is increasingly developing sustainable polymer solutions to meet evolving market demands for environmentally responsible materials.
