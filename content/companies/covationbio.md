## Overview

Covation Biomaterials is a specialty biobased materials company headquartered in Newark, Delaware, founded in 2022 following the acquisition of DuPont Biomaterials by the Huafon Group. The company delivers innovative, high-performance bio-based polymer solutions that reduce reliance on petroleum-derived materials across multiple industries.

Built on DuPont's legacy of polymer innovation, Covation Biomaterials combines established technology platforms with new product development to serve the growing demand for sustainable, renewable alternatives in consumer and industrial applications.

### Core Product Portfolio

**Sorona® Polymer:**
- High-performance polyester fiber
- 37% bio-based content from renewable feedstocks
- Applications: Apparel, activewear, performance fabrics, carpet fibers
- Properties: Durability, elasticity, and comfort comparable to synthetic alternatives

**Susterra® Plant-Based Building Block:**
- Derived from renewable plant resources
- Reduces petroleum dependency in downstream applications
- Applications: Footwear, outdoor apparel, coatings, inks, functional fluids
- Enables customers to achieve sustainability goals without performance compromise

**Zemea® Biodegradable Polymer:**
- Fully biodegradable material from renewable resources
- Applications: Packaging, flexible films, coatings
- Supports circular economy objectives for brands and converters

**BioPTMEG® (Bio-Based Polytetramethylene Ether Glycol):**
- Novel bio-based alternative to petroleum-derived PTMEG
- Launched at K-Show 2025
- Applications: Spandex, polyurethanes, thermoplastic elastomers
- Enables sustainable production of performance fibers and elastomers

### Market Segments

**Textiles & Apparel:**
- Performance and casual apparel requiring durability and comfort
- Athletic and swimwear applications
- Carpet and home textiles

**Beauty & Personal Care:**
- Cosmetics and skincare formulations
- Leveraging Susterra's bio-based benefits

**Packaging & Industrial:**
- Food and beverage packaging (Zemea)
- Protective coatings and inks
- Industrial fluids requiring sustainable alternatives

### Sustainability Focus

Covation Biomaterials helps customers:
- Reduce carbon footprint through bio-based feedstock sourcing
- Transition from petroleum dependency to renewable resources
- Meet consumer demand for sustainable products
- Achieve circular economy goals without compromising performance
