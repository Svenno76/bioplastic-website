Trevira GmbH, a subsidiary of Indorama Ventures, specializes in sustainable man-made fibers. The company produces PLA biopolymer fibers as a sustainable alternative to petroleum-based fibers, and has developed recycled flame-retardant Trevira CS fibers using textile waste. Trevira also works with sister company Schoeller GmbH to develop cable types from recycled materials for the automotive industry.
