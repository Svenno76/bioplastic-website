## Overview

ALPLA Werke Alwin Lehner GmbH & Co KG is a global leader in plastic packaging solutions headquartered in Hard, Austria. With 25,440 employees across 200 production sites in 46 countries and annual sales exceeding â‚¬5.2 billion, ALPLA specializes in blow-molded bottles, injection-molded parts, preforms, closures, and tubes for diverse industries. The company operates at the intersection of innovation and sustainability, developing advanced packaging solutions that address environmental challenges while meeting stringent performance requirements.

ALPLA has established itself as a sustainability leader through comprehensive recycling operations, development of bio-based alternatives, and pioneering work in paper bottle technology. The company's commitment to circular economy principles is demonstrated through multiple innovative initiatives including ALPLArecycling and Paboco paper bottle ventures.
