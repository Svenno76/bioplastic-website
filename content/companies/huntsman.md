## Overview

Huntsman Corporation is a major American specialty chemicals and advanced materials company headquartered in The Woodlands, Texas. With approximately 7,000 employees across more than 60 manufacturing, R&D, and operations facilities in over 25 countries, Huntsman is a leading producer of polyurethane systems, performance chemicals, and advanced materials serving diverse industrial sectors.

The company specializes in developing innovative chemical and material solutions addressing customer needs across polyurethanes, performance products, advanced materials, and chemical sectors. Huntsman maintains focus on operational excellence, customer collaboration, and continuous innovation.
