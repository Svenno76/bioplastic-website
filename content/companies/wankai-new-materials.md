## Overview

Wankai New Materials Co., Ltd. is a leading Chinese polyester producer and pioneering innovator in sustainable biopolymers, headquartered in Haining, Zhejiang Province. The company produces polyethylene terephthalate (PET) materials at commercial scale (3,000 MT/year) for packaging, textiles, and specialty applications. Wankai is strategically positioned at the forefront of circular economy innovation through a transformative joint venture with Carbios, a French biotechnology company, to establish enzymatic PET recycling technology in China—targeting 50,000 MT/year recycling capacity by 2026.

This partnership represents a significant convergence of Asian production scale with European biotech innovation, positioning Wankai as a critical player in the global transition to circular polyester and next-generation sustainable PET solutions. The company's SZSE:301216 listing on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange provides public market validation of its growth trajectory and strategic positioning.
