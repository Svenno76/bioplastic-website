## Overview

Angstrom Group, operating as Angstrom Technology, is a Canadian company headquartered in Ottawa, Ontario, that specializes in the design, manufacture, and installation of modular cleanroom systems. Founded in 1985, the company has built a reputation for delivering high-quality, customizable cleanroom solutions for industries that require precisely controlled manufacturing and research environments.

Cleanrooms are essential for many advanced manufacturing processes, including the production of bioplastics, pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and electronic components. Angstrom Technology's modular cleanroom systems provide the controlled environments necessary to ensure product quality, regulatory compliance, and process reliability.

## Core Products and Technologies

### Modular Cleanroom Systems

Angstrom Technology's modular cleanroom systems offer several advantages over traditional built-in-place cleanrooms:

- **Rapid deployment**: Modular construction significantly reduces installation time
- **Flexibility**: Systems can be reconfigured, expanded, or relocated as needs change
- **Cost-effectiveness**: Lower total cost of ownership compared to conventional cleanrooms
- **Quality assurance**: Factory-controlled manufacturing ensures consistent quality

### Cleanroom Types

**HardWall Cleanrooms**: Rigid, non-outgassing panels provide a sealed environment with precise pressure control. HardWall cleanrooms are suitable for ISO Class 5-7 applications requiring strict contamination control.

**RigidWall Cleanrooms**: A cost-effective alternative to HardWall systems, RigidWall cleanrooms use interlocking panel systems that provide excellent environmental control for ISO Class 6-8 applications.

**SoftWall Cleanrooms**: Flexible curtain-based systems for applications requiring basic contamination control. SoftWall cleanrooms are ideal for temporary or semi-permanent installations.

### Cleanroom Components

Angstrom Technology supplies a comprehensive range of cleanroom components:

- **HEPA and ULPA filtration**: High-efficiency particulate air filters for contamination control
- **Fan filter units**: Self-contained filtration modules for ceiling integration
- **Air showers**: Decontamination chambers for personnel and material entry
- **Pass-through chambers**: Secure transfer of materials between cleanroom zones
- **Environmental control systems**: HVAC, temperature, humidity, and pressure control
- **Monitoring and automation**: Real-time monitoring of cleanroom parameters

## Relevance to Bioplastics Manufacturing

Cleanroom technology plays an increasingly important role in the bioplastics industry, particularly for:

- **Medical-grade bioplastics**: Production of biopolymers for medical devices and pharmaceutical packaging requires controlled environments
- **Food-contact bioplastics**: Manufacturing of food-contact approved bioplastics often requires cleanroom conditions to ensure product safety
- **High-purity biopolymers**: Production of biopolymers for electronics and optical applications requires contamination-free manufacturing
- **Research and development**: Bioplastics R&D laboratories benefit from controlled environments for consistent testing and development

Angstrom Technology's cleanroom systems are designed and manufactured to meet international standards:
