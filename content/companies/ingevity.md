## Overview

Ingevity Corporation is a specialty materials and chemical manufacturer headquartered in North Charleston, South Carolina. Spun off from WestRock in 2014, Ingevity operates in 31 countries with 2,050+ employees, reporting through three business segments: Performance Materials, Advanced Polymer Technologies, and Performance Chemicals.

The company is the world's leading innovator in caprolactone technology, with 40 years of expertise in producing high-performance, sustainable, and biodegradable biopolymers for diverse applications.

### Core Business: Caprolactone Biopolymers

**Capa® Product Family:**
- Industry-leading caprolactone-based thermoplastics
- 40 years of innovation and market presence
- Fully biodegradable polymers from renewable and recycled feedstocks
- Performance-matched to conventional plastics
- Multiple certifications for biodegradability

**Product Variants:**
- **Capa™ Grade Series:** Various viscosity and performance grades
- **Custom Formulations:** Tailored polymer blends for specific applications
- **Specialty Compounds:** Blends with enhanced properties
