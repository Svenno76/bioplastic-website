## Overview

Gabriel-Chemie Group is a leading European producer of masterbatches, color concentrates, and polymer additives headquartered in Gumpoldskirchen, Austria. Founded in 1950, Gabriel-Chemie has spent over seven decades developing specialized additive solutions for the plastics industry, with a growing focus on bioplastics and sustainable material formulations.

The company combines traditional expertise with innovation, serving converter and manufacturer customers across Europe and globally with high-quality masterbatch and additive solutions.

### Core Business: Masterbatches & Additives

**Product Categories:**

**Color Masterbatches:**
- Black & white concentrates for polymer coloration
- Custom color development and matching
- Consistent quality across production runs
- Optimized for processing efficiency

**Functional Masterbatches:**
- UV protection additives (light and weathering stability)
- Flame retardancy (meeting safety standards)
- Antistatic properties (ESD protection)
- Laser marking and traceability additives
- Slip and antiblock agents

**Bioplastics Additives:**
- Specialized additives for PLA, PHA, and other biopolymers
- Processing aids optimized for bio-based polymers
- Performance enhancers maintaining biodegradability
- Durability improvements for bioplastic applications

**Sustainable Solutions:**
- Recycled carrier-based masterbatches
- Bio-based polymer concentrates
- Low-migration additives for food contact
- Environmentally friendly additive systems
