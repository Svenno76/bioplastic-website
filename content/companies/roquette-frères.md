## Overview

Roquette Frères is a global leader in plant-based ingredients and bioplastics, headquartered in Lestrem in the Hauts-de-France region. Founded in 1933, the company has evolved from a traditional starch processor into a diversified plant-based materials innovator with operations spanning five continents. With approximately 11,000 employees and annual revenues exceeding €3.8 billion, Roquette represents one of the world's top-10 bioplastics manufacturers by market presence and innovation capacity.

The company's commitment to sustainable, plant-based solutions has positioned it as a strategic player in the global transition toward circular bioeconomy principles. Roquette's extensive portfolio of plant-derived ingredients and biopolymers serves diverse industries including food, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, packaging, textiles, and automotive sectors.

## Starch-Based Bioplastics Platform

### Gaialene Brand and Product Range

Roquette's bioplastic products are marketed under the **Gaialene brand**, representing the company's comprehensive line of plant-based and biodegradable polymer solutions:

**Gaialene Biopolyester Series**:
- Biodegradable polyesters derived from plant sugars
- Industrial and home compostable grades
- Food contact compliant formulations
- Performance grades comparable to conventional polyesters

**Gaialene Polyol Series**:
- Bio-based polyols for polyurethane foam production
- Renewable alternatives to petroleum-derived polyols
- Applications in flexible and rigid foam systems
- Sustainability benefits through reduced fossil fuel dependency
