## Overview

Lenzing Group is a global leader in specialty cellulosic fiber production, headquartered in Lenzing, Austria, and founded in 1938. Publicly listed on the Vienna Stock Exchange (VIE:LNZ), Lenzing operates the world's largest integrated pulp and viscose fiber site and maintains global manufacturing facilities across multiple continents. With over 1,000 employees, Lenzing has established itself as a pioneer in sustainable fiber innovation, producing TENCEL™ Lyocell, LENZING™ Modal, and LENZING™ Viscose fibers that represent the highest standards in wood-based, fully biodegradable materials.

Lenzing's comprehensive portfolio of cellulosic fibers serves diverse industries from premium fashion and apparel to home textiles, nonwovens, hygiene products, and emerging food packaging applications. The company's commitment to circular economy principles, closed-loop production processes, and continuous sustainability innovation positions it as a global leader in environmentally responsible fiber production.
