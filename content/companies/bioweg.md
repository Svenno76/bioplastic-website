## Overview

BIOWEG is a purpose-driven biotechnology company founded in 2019, powered by conscious ChemDesign principles. The company specializes in using precision fermentation and green chemistry to create high-performance, bio-based, and biodegradable ingredients that replace intentionally added microplastics and fossil-derived polymers.

## Core Technologies and Innovations

- **Precision Fermentation**: Advanced fermentation using bioengineered microorganisms to produce bacterial cellulose
- **Sustainable Feedstocks**: Uses side-streams from food and agricultural industries (molasses, starch by-products)
- **Green Chemistry**: Sustainable processes minimizing environmental impact
- **Bio-Based Polymers**: Biodegradable alternatives to fossil-derived microplastics

## Primary Product Lines

1. **Micbeads**: Biodegradable micro-powders for cosmetics offering premium sensory performance
2. **RheoWeg**: Bio-based rheology and suspension control for personal and home care
3. **AgriWeg**: Seed and fertilizer coatings with controlled release properties

## Industry Focus and Applications

- **Cosmetics & Personal Care**: Toothpastes, body scrubs, face creams, beauty products
- **Home Care Products**: Laundry detergents, cleaners, household products
- **Agriculture**: Seed coatings and fertilizer formulations
- **Consumer Goods**: Microplastic replacement applications across industries

## Recent Achievements

- €16 million Series A financing (2024) led by Axeleo Capital's Green Tech Industry Fund
- €1.5 million additional funding (2025) for scale-up
- Partnership with Bayer for biodegradable seed coating development
- Demonstration facility in Quakenbrück, Germany
- Formulation lab on Bayer Crop Science campus in Monheim

## Strategic Focus

BIOWEG addresses the EU microplastic ban and global regulatory trends by providing superior performance alternatives to conventional microplastics, enabling brands to comply with regulations while maintaining product quality.
