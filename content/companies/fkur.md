## Overview

FKuR Kunststoff GmbH is a leading German developer and manufacturer of bio-based, biodegradable, and recyclable plastic compounds headquartered in Willich, North Rhine-Westphalia. Founded in 2003 as an incorporated entity (with research roots extending to 1992), FKuR has established itself as a specialized bioplastics compound manufacturer serving diverse markets with innovative sustainable polymer solutions. With approximately 50-150 employees and operations spanning Europe, North America, and Asia, FKuR provides customized bioplastic formulations supporting global customers' sustainability objectives.

The company's extensive portfolio of biopolymer compounds—encompassing PLA, PHA, bio-based polyethylene, starch-based materials, and natural fiber composites—combined with 20+ years of bioplastics development experience and partnerships with leading research institutions, positions FKuR as a trusted innovation partner in the global bioplastics industry.
