## Overview

Northern Technologies International Corporation (NTIC) is a specialty materials and chemicals manufacturer headquartered in Bloomington, Minnesota. Founded in 1972, NTIC is a leading developer of bio-based and biodegradable polymer resin compounds. The company's Natur-Tec® brand is the flagship line of biobased, compostable plastics engineered to replace conventional petroleum-based plastics with 100% certified biodegradable and compostable alternatives.

### Core Business: Natur-Tec Bioplastics

**Natur-Tec Brand Overview:**
- Proprietary formulations of biodegradable polymers and natural materials
- 100% biobased, non-toxic alternatives to conventional plastics
- No polyethylene, polypropylene, or polystyrene content
- Patented ReX Process manufacturing technology
- Global manufacturing and R&D capabilities

**Material Composition:**
- Biodegradable polymer blends
- Natural material reinforcement
- Proprietary stabilization technology
- Food-contact compliant formulations
- Cost-competitive pricing
