Blue Circle Olefins secured funding to advance ProjectNL, commercial Methanol to Olefins production facility in Rotterdam, Netherlands. Project aims to produce sustainable olefins from renewable methanol.

## Funding Details

This investment supports Blue Circle Olefins's growth plans and technology development in bio-based materials.

## Strategic Use

The capital will be deployed to scale operations and advance the company's bioplastics technology platform.
