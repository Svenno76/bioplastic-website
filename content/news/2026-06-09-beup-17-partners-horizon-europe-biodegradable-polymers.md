17 partners from 9 countries have united in the new Horizon Europe-funded project Be-UP to boost the industrial uptake of biodegradable polymers across Europe.

The consortium brings together leading research institutions, industry partners, and SMEs to accelerate the development and commercialization of biodegradable polymer solutions. The project addresses key barriers to industrial adoption, including cost competitiveness, performance optimization, and end-of-life management.

This initiative represents a significant step forward in the EU's strategy to transition toward a circular bioeconomy, supporting the European Green Deal's objectives for reducing plastic waste and promoting sustainable materials.
