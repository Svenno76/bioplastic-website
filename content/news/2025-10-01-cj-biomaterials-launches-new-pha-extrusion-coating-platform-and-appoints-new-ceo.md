CJ Biomaterials, the bioplastics arm of South Korean conglomerate CJ CheilJedang, has announced multiple significant developments that expand the application range of its PHACT PHA biopolymer.

### New PHA Extrusion Coating Platform

CJ Biomaterials has launched a new PHA platform specifically engineered for extrusion coating applications. The technology enables PHA coatings on paper and board substrates, replacing polyethylene and other fossil-based barrier coatings that currently make paper packaging difficult to recycle. This opens up significant new markets in food serviceware, packaging, and industrial coating applications.

### PHACT Masterbatch

The company also introduced a new PHACT masterbatch product, making it easier for converters and brand owners to incorporate PHACT PHA into existing manufacturing processes without requiring specialized equipment or expertise.

### Strategic Appointments and Partnerships

Allan Chung has been appointed CEO of CJ Biomaterials to lead the company's next phase of growth and commercial expansion. The company separately partnered with BIQ Materials to produce artificial turf infill made with PHACT PHA for the European market, demonstrating the versatility of PHA in non-traditional applications.

### Broader CJ Bio Strategy

CJ CheilJedang won the Grand Prize in Marine Ecosystem Conservation at the 2025 SDGs Awards, recognizing the company's contributions to ocean health through its bioplastics and PHACT marine-biodegradable products.
