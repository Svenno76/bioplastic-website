**Essen, June 10, 2026** — Evonik Industries has launched the **Purocel™ series**, a comprehensive portfolio of specialized adsorbents and hydrotreating catalysts designed to purify pyrolysis oil derived from mixed plastic waste. The new product line addresses the single greatest technical barrier to scaling chemical recycling: converting heterogeneous, contaminated pyrolysis oil into a consistent, high-quality feedstock suitable for steam crackers.

## The Chemical Recycling Bottleneck

Chemical recycling via pyrolysis — thermally decomposing mixed plastic waste in the absence of oxygen to produce pyrolysis oil — is widely recognized as essential for achieving circularity in plastics. However, the oil produced from real-world waste streams contains problematic impurities:

| Contaminant | Source | Impact on Steam Cracker |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| **Chlorides (Cl)** | PVC, flame retardants | Corrosion, catalyst poisoning |
| **Metals (Na, K, Ca, Fe, Zn)** | Additives, fillers, contamination | Fouling, catalyst deactivation |
| **Nitrogen compounds** | Nylon, polyurethanes | Ammonia formation, NOx emissions |
| **Oxygenates** | PET, polyesters, contamination | Instability, gum formation |
| **Silicon** | Fillers, adhesives | Refractory deposits |
| **Dienes/olefins** | Thermal cracking products | Gum formation, fouling |

Without effective purification, pyrolysis oil cannot meet the stringent feedstock specifications of modern steam crackers — typically **<1 ppm chlorides, <0.5 ppm metals, <50 ppm nitrogen**.

## The Purocel™ Solution: Integrated Purification Train

Evonik's Purocel™ series provides a **modular, multi-stage purification approach** that can be tailored to specific waste stream compositions and target specifications:

### Purocel™ Adsorbents (Fixed-Bed Polishing)

| Product | Target Contaminants | Key Features |
|---------|---------------------|--------------|
| **Purocel 505** | Chlorides (HCl, organic Cl) | High-capacity chloride scavenging, robust to moisture |
| **Purocel 510** | Metals (Na, K, Ca, Fe, Zn) | Selective metal chelation, regenerable |
| **Purocel 515** | Silicon, phosphorus, arsenic | Specialty adsorbent for refractory elements |

### Purocel™ Hydrotreating Catalysts (Catalytic Upgrading)

| Product | Function | Key Features |
|---------|----------|--------------|
| **Purocel HTC-1** | Hydrodechlorination, hydrogenation | Converts organic Cl to HCl for adsorbent capture; saturates dienes/olefins |
| **Purocel HTC-2** | Hydrodenitrogenation, hydrodesulfurization | Deep N/S removal; stable under high contaminant loads |
| **Purocel HTC-3** | Guard catalyst | Protects downstream catalysts from residual metals/silicon |

### Integrated Process Flow

```
Pyrolysis Oil → [Purocel HTC-1: Hydrodechlorination/Hydrogenation] 
              → [Purocel 505: Chloride Polishing] 
              → [Purocel HTC-2: HDN/HDS] 
              → [Purocel 510/515: Metal/Si Polishing] 
              → Steam Cracker-Ready Feedstock
```

This sequence achieves:
- **Chlorides**: <0.5 ppm (from 100-5,000 ppm typical)
- **Total metals**: <0.1 ppm (from 10-500 ppm typical)
- **Nitrogen**: <10 ppm (from 500-3,000 ppm typical)
- **Olefins/dienes**: Saturated for stability
- **Oxygenates**: Hydrodeoxygenated

## Technical Differentiation

### 1. **Synergistic Adsorbent-Catalyst Design**
Unlike standalone adsorbents or catalysts, Purocel™ products are co-developed to work in sequence. The hydrotreating catalysts convert reactive contaminants (organic chlorides, dienes) into forms that the adsorbents can efficiently capture, while the adsorbents protect the catalysts from poisoning.

### 2. **Regenerability and Circular Operation**
Purocel 505 and 510 adsorbents are designed for **thermal regeneration in situ**, enabling cyclic operation without continuous consumable replacement — critical for economic viability at scale.

### 3. **Feedstock Flexibility**
The modular design accommodates varying waste compositions (packaging-dominated, automotive shredder residue, WEEE plastics, agricultural films) by adjusting bed sizing and sequence.

### 4. **Drop-in Compatibility**
Purocel™ catalysts fit standard fixed-bed hydrotreating reactors; adsorbents use conventional adsorption vessels. No proprietary reactor technology required.
