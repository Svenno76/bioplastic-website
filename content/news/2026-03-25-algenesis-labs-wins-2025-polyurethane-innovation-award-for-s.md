## A Milestone in Bio-Based Polymers

Algenesis Labs has officially been recognized for its groundbreaking work in the bioplastics sector, taking home the highly coveted 2025 Polyurethane Innovation Award. The accolade highlights the company’s proprietary Soleic® technology, a pioneering bio-based polyurethane (PU) formulation that directly challenges the heavily fossil-reliant plastics industry. 

Traditional polyurethanes are ubiquitous—found in everything from footwear midsoles and surfboards to automotive interiors and insulation foams. However, their petrochemical origins and complex cross-linked molecular structures make them notoriously difficult to recycle, leading to long-term environmental accumulation and microplastic pollution. Algenesis Labs addresses this critical bottleneck with Soleic®, demonstrating that high-performance elastomers and foams can be derived entirely from renewable, plant- and algae-based carbon.

## Technical Breakthroughs with Soleic® Technology

Soleic® technology represents a fundamental shift in polyurethane chemistry. By utilizing oils extracted from algae and non-food plants, Algenesis synthesizes the bio-polyols and other vital monomers required for PU production. Importantly, the resulting material is meticulously engineered to be fully biodegradable in natural environments at the end of its lifecycle. When disposed of, Soleic® products are consumed by naturally occurring microorganisms, effectively eliminating the generation of persistent microplastics.

The 2025 Polyurethane Innovation Award validates not only the environmental credentials of Soleic® but also its commercial viability. Achieving parity with petroleum-based polyurethanes in durability, flexibility, tear strength, and load-bearing capacity has long been a technical hurdle for bioplastic producers. Algenesis Labs has successfully bridged this gap, offering drop-in solutions that manufacturers can seamlessly integrate without sacrificing end-product performance.

## Looking Ahead

This industry recognition serves as a strong catalyst for Algenesis Labs as the company seeks to scale its manufacturing capabilities and expand into new consumer and industrial markets. As global regulatory pressures mount against persistent plastics and major brands actively seek decarbonized material supply chains, Soleic® is positioned to play a crucial role in the broader transition toward a circular materials economy.
