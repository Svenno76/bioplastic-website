Emirates Biotech is making rapid progress on what aims to be the world's largest bioplastics production facility, with key strategic partnerships and contracts now in place.

### Strategic Equipment Partnership

Emirates Biotech and Sulzer have advanced their strategic partnership through an equipment supply contract for the landmark PLA production facility in the UAE. Sulzer, a global leader in fluid engineering and polymer processing technology, will supply critical equipment for the plant's polymerization and processing operations.

### Main Contractor Appointed

Samsung E&A has been selected as the main contractor for the project, bringing large-scale industrial construction expertise to what will be a world-first megascale bioplastics facility in the Gulf region.

### Producing PLA from Food Waste

The facility will use feedstock from the European CIRCLE Consortium to produce PLA from food waste — an innovative approach that combines waste valorization with bioplastics production. This addresses both the UAE's food waste challenge and the growing global demand for sustainable biomaterials.

### UAE Leadership in Bioplastics

The project represents a significant strategic investment by the UAE in establishing domestic bioplastics production capacity, reducing reliance on imported materials and supporting the nation's sustainability and circular economy goals.
