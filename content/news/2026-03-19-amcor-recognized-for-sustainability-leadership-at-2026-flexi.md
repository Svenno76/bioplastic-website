## Industry Recognition for Bio-based Packaging

Global packaging leader Amcor has been honored for its sustainability leadership at the 2026 Flexible Packaging Association (FPA) Achievement Awards. The prestigious recognition centers on the company’s development and commercialization of Earth Sense Pro, a next-generation flexible bioplastic packaging solution designed to dramatically reduce the industry's reliance on fossil-based plastics.

The FPA Achievement Awards annually highlight breakthrough material technologies that push the boundaries of both packaging performance and environmental stewardship. Amcor’s top-tier placement in the 2026 cycle underscores the flexible packaging industry's accelerating shift toward advanced bio-based alternatives that do not compromise on functional performance or manufacturing efficiency.

## The Earth Sense Pro Advantage

Earth Sense Pro represents a significant technical milestone in the realm of flexible bioplastics. Engineered utilizing advanced bio-based polymers, the packaging film maintains the stringent oxygen and moisture barrier properties strictly required for perishable food, pharmaceutical, and healthcare applications. Historically, achieving high-barrier performance and high puncture resistance in flexible bioplastics has been a critical challenge for compounders and film extruders. 

By integrating next-generation bio-polymers and proprietary compatibilizers, Amcor has successfully formulated a material that extends product shelf life while offering an optimized end-of-life profile. Earth Sense Pro drastically lowers the overall greenhouse gas footprint compared to conventional multi-layer metallized flexible structures and integrates seamlessly into existing high-speed packaging lines.

## Moving Toward a Circular Economy

The FPA’s recognition of Amcor highlights the vital role that sophisticated material science plays in achieving global decarbonization targets. As fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) companies face mounting regulatory pressure to adopt eco-friendly packaging, scalable bioplastic films like Earth Sense Pro offer a commercially viable pathway to sustainable supply chains. 

Amcor’s continued strategic investment in bio-based material research demonstrates a clear, long-term commitment to replacing traditional petrochemical films, solidifying its position at the forefront of the circular economy transition.
