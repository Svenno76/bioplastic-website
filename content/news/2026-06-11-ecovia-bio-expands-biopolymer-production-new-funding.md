Ecovia Bio is expanding its biopolymer production capacity following a new funding round. The company, known for its plant-based resin solutions, is scaling up to meet growing demand for sustainable alternatives to conventional plastics.

The expansion will enable Ecovia Bio to increase production volumes and accelerate the commercialization of its bio-based polymer portfolio across multiple industries including packaging, agriculture, and consumer goods.
