Thermoset resin producer Bakelite acquired Sestec, Polish company specializing in sustainable protein-based adhesives for wood and composite products, expanding its bio-based materials portfolio.

## Deal Structure

The acquisition represents a strategic move for Bakelite to expand its portfolio and market presence in the bioplastics industry.

## Strategic Implications

This merger and acquisition activity reflects the ongoing consolidation in the bioplastics sector as companies seek to achieve scale and broaden their technology platforms.
