The global bioplastics market is witnessing significant growth as industries and consumers increasingly shift toward sustainable materials. According to Ceresana's latest market report, the market is projected to reach $18.7 billion by 2031.

### Key Findings

The biodegradable plastics segment is showing the strongest growth, driven by regulatory pressure, corporate sustainability commitments, and consumer demand for eco-friendly alternatives. The report highlights that bioplastics are moving from niche applications to mainstream adoption across packaging, agriculture, automotive, and consumer goods.

### Regional Trends

Europe continues to lead in bioplastics adoption, supported by the European Green Deal and the Single-Use Plastics Directive. Asia-Pacific is the fastest-growing region, driven by large-scale production capacity expansions in China and Southeast Asia.
