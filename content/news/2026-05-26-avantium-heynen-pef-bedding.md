**AMSTERDAM, Netherlands** — **Avantium N.V.** and **Heynen Systems B.V.** have announced a collaboration to explore applications of **releaf® PEF (polyethylene furanoate)** in **bedding and upholstery**, developing prototype comfort foams and technical textiles based on Avantium's 100% plant-based, recyclable polymer.

The partnership combines Avantium's polymer technology with Heynen Systems' expertise in **technical textile knitting**, **interliner automation**, and **comfort foam lamination** — targeting the **Interzum Forum Italy** event (Bergamo, June 4–5, 2026) for first prototype showcase.

## Collaboration Scope

| Avantium Contribution | Heynen Systems Contribution |
|----------------------|----------------------------|
| releaf® PEF polymer grades (injection, extrusion, foam) | Technical textile knitting (warp/weft/circular) |
| Polymer processing support | Interliner automation & lamination |
| LCA data for bedding applications | Prototype development & testing |
| Recyclability validation | Pilot production at Heynen facility (NL) |

## Target Applications

### 1. **Comfort Foam Cores**
- **PEF-based polyol** → polyurethane foam for mattress cores & upholstery padding
- **Target properties**: Durability, resilience, breathability, reduced VOC emissions
- **Circularity**: Designed for chemical recycling (glycolysis) at end-of-life

### 2. **Technical Textile Covers**
- **PEF fiber/spunbond** → mattress tickings, upholstery fabrics
- **Knitted structures** with zoned ventilation, moisture management
- **Integrated interliners** for fire retardancy (without halogenated FRs)

### 3. **Hybrid Laminates**
- PEF foam + PEF textile → monomaterial comfort systems
- **Design for recycling**: Single-polymer construction enables mechanical recycling

## Sustainability Drivers for Bedding/Upholstery

| Driver | PEF Advantage |
|--------|---------------|
| **EU ESPR (Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation)** | Recyclable, biobased content, digital product passport ready |
| **Mattress EPR schemes** (France, Netherlands, Belgium) | Monomaterial design simplifies EoL sorting & recycling |
| **Hotel/Contract market** | Carbon footprint reduction claims (LCA-backed) |
| **VOC/Indoor air quality** | Low-emission potential vs. conventional PU foams |

## Interzum Forum Italy 2026 Showcase

Prototypes to be displayed at **Heynen Systems booth** (Hall B, Stand 12):
- **PEF comfort foam mattress core** (180 × 200 cm)
- **Knitted PEF mattress cover** with integrated climate zones
- **Upholstery panel** with PEF foam + textile laminate
- **LCA comparison**: PEF vs. conventional PU/polyester systems

> "Bedding and upholstery are high-volume, long-life applications where PEF's combination of **biobased origin, durability, and recyclability** creates unique value," said **Gert-Jan Gruter, CTO of Avantium**. "Heynen's textile automation capability is the perfect complement to our polymer platform."

> "We see strong demand from mattress OEMs for **circular material systems** that don't compromise on comfort or processing," said **Jan Heynen, CEO of Heynen Systems**. "PEF's thermal stability and melt strength open new design possibilities for knitted technical textiles."

## Technical Background: releaf® PEF

| Property | Value | Relevance to Bedding |
|----------|-------|---------------------|
| **Biobased carbon** | 100% (ASTM D6866) | Renewable content claims |
| **Tg (glass transition)** | ~86°C | Thermal stability in comfort foam |
| **Melting point** | ~235°C | Processing window for foam/textile |
| **O₂ barrier** | 10× better than PET | Potential for modified atmosphere packaging of rolled mattresses |
| **Mechanical recycling** | Demonstrated at pilot scale | Circularity pathway |
| **Chemical recycling** | Glycolysis to BHET + FDCA | Monomer recovery |
