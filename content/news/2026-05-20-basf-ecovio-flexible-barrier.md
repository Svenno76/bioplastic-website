**LUDWIGSHAFEN, Germany** — **BASF** has launched an expanded **ecovio® portfolio** for flexible packaging, introducing **multiple barrier structures** based on its certified home-compostable biopolymer that can be applied to both **paper and plastic substrates** — with end-of-life options via **organic recycling (home/industrial composting)** or **paper recycling (repulpable)**.

The launch, previewed at **interpack 2026** (Düsseldorf, May 7–13), addresses a critical gap in sustainable packaging: the ability to combine **high barrier performance** (oxygen, moisture, aroma) with **certified compostability** and **recyclability in existing streams**.

## Portfolio Expansion

The new ecovio® grades enable three distinct packaging architectures:

| Architecture | Substrate | Barrier Layer | End-of-Life | Target Applications |
|--------------|-----------|---------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Plastic-based** | ecovio® film | ecovio® extrusion coating | Home/industrial composting (EN 13432, AS 5810, NF T 51-800) | Coffee, snacks, pet food, produce bags |
| **Paper-based (repulpable)** | Paper | ecovio® dispersion coating | Paper recycling (PTS-RH 021/97) + compostable | Dry food, confectionery, non-food |
| **Multilayer laminate** | Paper/film | ecovio® adhesive lamination | Composting (where infrastructure exists) | High-barrier requirements (O₂ < 1 cm³/m²·day) |

## Technical Performance

**Barrier properties** (typical values, 20°C/65% r.H.):

| Parameter | ecovio® Coating | Benchmark (PVdC) |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Oxygen transmission rate (OTR)** | < 5 cm³/m²·day·bar | < 1 cm³/m²·day·bar |
| **Water vapor transmission rate (WVTR)** | < 5 g/m²·day | < 1 g/m²·day |
| **Aroma retention** | Excellent (limonene test) | Excellent |

**Processing advantages**:
- **Co-extrusion & extrusion coating** on standard lines (180–220°C)
- **Water-based dispersion coating** for paper (rod, gravure, flexo)
- **Adhesive lamination** with compostable adhesives
- **Heat sealable** across broad temperature window (90–160°C)

## Regulatory & Certification

- **Home compostable**: TÜV Austria OK compost HOME, DIN CERTCO
- **Industrial compostable**: EN 13432, ISO 17088, AS 4736, ASTM D6400
- **Paper recyclability**: PTS-RH 021/97 (repulpability > 95% fiber recovery)
- **Food contact**: EU 10/2011, FDA FCN, China GB 4806
- **Biobased content**: Up to 70% (ASTM D6866), varying by grade

## Market Context

Flexible packaging represents **~40% of global plastic packaging by weight** but **< 5% recycling rate** due to multilayer complexity, contamination, and lack of collection. Compostable flexible packaging offers a **systemic solution** where organic recycling infrastructure exists (e.g., Italy, Germany, Benelux, parts of France, Australia).

> "The new ecovio® portfolio gives converters and brand owners a **toolkit, not a single grade**," said **Dr. Dirk Voeste, Senior Vice President, BASF Performance Materials**. "They can match barrier needs to substrate choice and local end-of-life infrastructure — composting, paper recycling, or both."

## interpack 2026 Showcase

BASF demonstrated:
- **Coffee pouch**: Paper/ecovio® with aroma barrier, repulpable
- **Snack bag**: ecovio® film with O₂/Moisture barrier, home compostable
- **Produce bag**: ecovio® blown film, industrial compostable, anti-fog
- **Lamination**: PET/ecovio®/PE structure with compostable adhesive
