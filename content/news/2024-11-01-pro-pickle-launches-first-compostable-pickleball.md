Pro-Pickle introduced Compost-a-Ball, first compostable pickleball ball designed to break down in 100 days, addressing environmental concerns in rapidly growing pickleball industry.

## Product Innovation

Pro-Pickle's new offering addresses market demand for sustainable alternatives with improved performance characteristics.

## Applications

The product targets key applications in packaging, consumer goods, and other sectors seeking bio-based material solutions.
