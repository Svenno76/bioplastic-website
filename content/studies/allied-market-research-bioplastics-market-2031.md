## Executive Summary

According to Allied Market Research's latest report "Bioplastics Market by Type, Application, and Region: Global Opportunity Analysis and Industry Forecast, 2022–2031," the global bioplastics market was valued at **$6.3 billion in 2021** and is projected to reach **$18.7 billion by 2031**, growing at a **CAGR of 11.7%** during the forecast period.

## Key Findings

### Market Drivers

- **Regulatory pressure**: The EU Single-Use Plastics Directive and Extended Producer Responsibility requirements are accelerating adoption of bioplastics across packaging applications.
- **Corporate sustainability commitments**: Major CPG companies have pledged to transition to bio-based and biodegradable materials.
- **Consumer demand**: Growing awareness of plastic pollution is driving consumer preference for eco-friendly alternatives.
- **Technological advances**: Improvements in biopolymer chemistry are enhancing performance and reducing cost gaps versus conventional plastics.

### Market Segmentation

The report segments the bioplastics market by:

- **Type**: Biodegradable (PLA, PHA, starch blends, others) and Non-biodegradable (Bio-PE, Bio-PET, Bio-PA, others)
- **Application**: Packaging, Agriculture, Consumer Goods, Automotive & Transportation, Textiles, and Others
- **Region**: North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and LAMEA

### Regional Outlook

**Asia-Pacific** is expected to dominate the market during the forecast period, driven by large-scale production capacity expansions in China, India, and Southeast Asia. **Europe** follows closely, supported by the European Green Deal and stringent single-use plastic regulations.
