## Event Overview

The Packaging Recycling Summit 2026 brings together leaders across the entire packaging recycling value chain. The summit is designed as a collaborative forum where brand owners, material suppliers, reprocessors, retailers, and policymakers work together to advance circular economy solutions for packaging.

- **Event Type**: Industry Summit
- **Date**: June 15-17, 2026
