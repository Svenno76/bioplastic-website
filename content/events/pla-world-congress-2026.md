## Event Overview

The PLA World Congress is the definitive global platform for polylactic acid (PLA) bioplastics. The 9th edition brings together researchers, manufacturers, converters, and brand owners to discuss the full spectrum of PLA applications, from packaging and fibers to advanced applications in automotive and electronics.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Conference
- **Date**: June 2-3, 2026
- **Location**: Munich, Germany
- **Edition**: 9th PLA World Congress
- **Format**: Hybrid (in-person and online participation available)
- **Organizer**: Renewable Carbon Plastics / bioplastics MAGAZINE
- **Website**: [PLA World Congress](https://www.bioplasticsmagazine.com/)

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **PLA Production Technologies**: Manufacturing processes, capacity expansions, and process innovations
- **Feedstock & Sustainability**: Lactic acid production, renewable feedstocks, and sustainability metrics
- **Packaging Applications**: Food packaging, beverage bottles, flexible films, and rigid containers
- **Fiber & Non-Woven Applications**:
  - Spunbond and meltblown nonwovens
  - Technical and industrial textiles
  - Apparel and fashion applications
- **Automotive & Transportation**:
  - Interior components
  - Lightweight applications
  - Performance requirements
- **Consumer Electronics & Durables**:
  - Electronic enclosures
  - Appliance components
  - Consumer goods
- **Medical & Healthcare**: Biocompatibility, sterilization, and regulatory considerations
- **Recycling & End-of-Life**:
  - Chemical and mechanical recycling
  - Composting and biodegradation
  - Circular economy strategies
- **Future Prospects & Limitations**: Market challenges, technical limitations, and emerging opportunities

- Increasing capacity expansions from major producers
