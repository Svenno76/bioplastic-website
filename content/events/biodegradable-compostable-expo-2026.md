## Event Overview

The Biodegradable & Compostable Expo 2026 is India's premier B2B platform dedicated exclusively to biodegradable and compostable products. The expo brings together the entire value chain from raw material suppliers to finished product manufacturers, serving the rapidly growing Indian and South Asian sustainable packaging markets.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: Trade Exhibition
- **Date**: June 4-6, 2026
- **Location**: India Expo Centre & Mart, Greater Noida, India
- **Website**: [Biodegradable & Compostable Expo](https://biodegradableexpo.com/)
- **Focus Markets**: India and South Asia

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Biodegradable Products**: Complete biodegradable product range and solutions
- **Compostable Materials**: EN 13432 and ASTM D6400 certified materials
- **Sustainable Packaging**: Food service items, flexible packaging, rigid containers
- **Product-Making Machinery**: Equipment for manufacturing biodegradable products
- **Raw Materials & Additives**: Biopolymers, processing aids, colorants, fillers
- **Food-Contact Compliance**: Regulatory requirements for food packaging
- **Certification & Testing**: Standards, certifications, quality assurance
- **Agricultural Films**: Biodegradable mulch films and agricultural applications
- **Market Trends**: Growth drivers in biodegradable products, regulatory developments

India is a key growth market for biodegradable products driven by:
- Single-use plastic bans and restrictions
- Growing environmental awareness
- Large population driving volume demand
- Government "Make in India" initiatives
- Renewable feedstock availability (sugarcane, tapioca)
- Emerging skilled manufacturing base
