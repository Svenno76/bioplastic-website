## Event Overview

InterPlas Thailand 2026 is the region's leading trade fair for plastics and polymer manufacturers, processors, and suppliers. The fair features dedicated Green Zone showcasing bioplastics products, sustainable packaging solutions, and environmentally-friendly manufacturing technologies.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Trade Fair
- **Date**: June 17-20, 2026
- **Location**: Bangkok, Thailand (BITEC)
- **Focus**: Plastics, polymers, and sustainable solutions
- **Special Feature**: Green Zone for bioplastics and sustainability

## Exhibition Sections

### Main Exhibition Areas
- **Plastics & Polymers**: General plastic materials and compounds
- **Equipment & Machinery**: Processing and manufacturing equipment
- **Additives & Chemicals**: Processing aids and colorants

- **Bioplastic Materials**: PLA, PHA, bio-PE, and other biopolymers
