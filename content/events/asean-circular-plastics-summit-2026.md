## Event Overview

The 5th ASEAN Circular Plastics Summit brings together industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators from Southeast Asia to address the region's critical plastic waste challenges and transition toward a circular economy. This summit is particularly focused on the unique challenges and opportunities in the ASEAN region.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: Regional Summit
- **Date**: March 12-13, 2026
- **Location**: Jakarta, Indonesia
- **Focus Region**: Southeast Asia (ASEAN countries)
- **Edition**: 5th annual summit
- **Website**: [ASEAN Circular Plastics Summit](https://www.eco-business.com/events/)

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Mechanical & Chemical Recycling Trends**: Latest technologies for plastic recovery and processing in ASEAN region
- **Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) Implementation**: EPR policy development in Indonesia and other ASEAN nations, regulatory frameworks
- **Food-Contact-Approved PET Recycling Systems**: Quality requirements for food packaging applications, regulatory compliance, certifications
- **Advanced Sorting Technologies**: Automated sorting innovations, contamination reduction, material identification
- **Single-Use Plastics Regulation**: Implementation of bans and restrictions across ASEAN nations
- **Circular Economy Infrastructure**: Development of collection and processing networks in Southeast Asia
- **Regional Market Dynamics**: ASEAN-specific supply and demand for recycled materials
- **Plastic Waste Management**: Landfill alternatives, waste-to-energy solutions, community-based approaches
- **International Standards & Certification**: ISO standards, regional certification requirements
