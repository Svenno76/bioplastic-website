## Event Overview

EUBCE 2026 is the 34th edition of the European Biomass Conference and Exhibition, Europe's leading platform for biomass energy, biorefining, and bio-based materials. The conference brings together researchers, policymakers, and industry leaders to discuss developments in sustainable biomass utilization and the circular bioeconomy.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Conference & Exhibition
- **Date**: May 19-22, 2026
- **Location**: The Hague, Netherlands
- **Organizer**: ETA-Florence Renewable Energies
- **Website**: [EUBCE 2026](https://www.eubce.com/)
- **Edition**: 34th edition
- **Expected Attendance**: 1,500+ participants from 60+ countries

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Biomass Valorization**: Sustainable utilization of biomass resources
- **Biorefining Technologies**: Conversion of biomass into chemicals, materials, and fuels
- **Biopolymers & Biomaterials**: Development and applications of bio-based polymers
- **Bioenergy**: Biofuels, biogas, and sustainable energy production
- **Circular Bioeconomy**: Integration of biomass into circular economy models
- **Sustainable Resource Management**: Responsible sourcing and feedstock strategies
- **Bio-based Products Development**: Commercial applications of biomass derivatives
- **Policy & Sustainability**: Regulatory frameworks and sustainability metrics
