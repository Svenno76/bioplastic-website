## Event Overview

The European Bioplastics Conference (EBC26) is the flagship annual event for the European bioplastics industry. Since its inception, the EBC has been the leading platform for industry exchange, attracting participation from across the entire bioplastics value chain.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Conference
- **Date**: December 1-2, 2026
- **Location**: Berlin, Germany
- **Organizer**: European Bioplastics e.V. (EUBP)
- **Website**: [European Bioplastics Conference](https://www.european-bioplastics.org/events/ebc/)
- **Expected Attendance**: 320+ participants

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Market Analysis**: Latest bioplastics market data and industry trends
- **Policy & Regulations**: EU plastic regulations, circular economy directives, and sustainability standards
- **Production Technologies**: Advances in bioplastic manufacturing and processing
- **Applications**: Packaging, automotive, textiles, agriculture, and consumer goods applications
- **Circular Economy**: End-of-life solutions, recycling innovations, and biodegradability standards
- **Feedstock Sustainability**: Responsible sourcing and biomass utilization
- **Future Prospects**: Innovation roadmaps and emerging biopolymer technologies

## About the Organizer

**European Bioplastics e.V. (EUBP)** is the leading trade association representing the bioplastics industry in Europe. Founded in 1993 and headquartered in Berlin, EUBP represents approximately 70 member companies throughout the EU, including:

- Agricultural feedstock producers
- Chemical and plastics manufacturers
- Industrial users and converters
- Recycling companies
- Research institutions

EUBP is committed to advancing the development, manufacturing, and application of bioplastics and bio-based polymers in a sustainable manner.
