## Event Overview

Interpack 2026 is the world's largest and most important trade fair for packaging technology, packaging materials, and processing machinery. This biennial event brings together the entire global packaging industry with a strong focus on innovation, sustainability, and circular economy solutions.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Trade Fair & Exhibition
- **Date**: May 7-13, 2026
- **Location**: Düsseldorf, Germany
- **Organizer**: Koelnmesse
- **Website**: [Interpack](https://www.interpack.com/)
- **Expected Attendance**: 150,000+ international visitors
- **Exhibitors**: 1,000+ global companies

## Main Topics & Focus Areas

- **Innovative Materials**: Monomaterials, bio-based solutions (bioplastics), Design for Recycling (DfR)
- **Smart Manufacturing**: Industry 4.0 technologies, automation, digital solutions for packaging
- **Sustainability**: Circular packaging solutions, carbon footprint reduction, sustainable sourcing
- **Processing Technologies**: Filling, sealing, labeling, converting equipment
- **Packaging Design**: Functional packaging, protective solutions, brand presentation
- **End-of-Life Solutions**: Recyclability, composting design, waste reduction
- **Materials Science**: Barrier technologies, lightweight solutions, performance materials
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Food safety standards, migration testing, sustainability regulations
