## Event Overview

Plastics Recycling Show Europe (PRSE) 2026 is Europe's premier event dedicated exclusively to plastics recycling. The exhibition and conference bring together the entire recycling value chain, from designers and producers creating recyclable materials to collection systems, sorting facilities, and reprocessors transforming used plastics into valuable secondary materials.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: Trade Fair & Conference
- **Date**: May 5-6, 2026
- **Location**: Amsterdam, Netherlands
- **Website**: [PRSE 2026](https://www.prseventeurope.com/)
- **Expected Exhibitors**: 450+ companies
- **Focus**: Plastic waste recycling and circular economy

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Plastics Recycling Technologies**: Sorting, separation, and processing equipment
- **Circular Economy Policy**: EU regulations, EPR implementation, sustainability targets
- **Recycling Innovations**: Advanced technologies for material recovery
- **Material Design for Recycling**: Design principles enabling recycling
- **Chemical & Advanced Recycling**: Depolymerization and chemical processes
- **Mechanical Recycling Advancements**: Quality improvements and processing innovations
- **Recycled Polymer Applications**: Use of recycled materials in new products
- **Collection Infrastructure**: Waste management systems and collection networks
- **Bioplastics in Recycling**: Integration of bioplastics into recycling streams
