## Event Overview

The AMI Bioplastics Conference is the third edition of the premier platform dedicated to the North American bioplastics industry. This conference provides an unparalleled opportunity to explore the entire bioplastics value chain, from material innovation to practical applications.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Conference
- **Date**: September 1-2, 2026
- **Location**: Cleveland, Ohio, USA
- **Organizer**: AMI (Applied Market Information)
- **Website**: [AMI Events - Bioplastics](https://www.ami-events.com/)
- **Focus Region**: North America
- **Edition**: Third edition (inaugural editions: 2021, 2024)

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Bio-based Polymers**: PLA, PHAs, bio-PE, bio-PET, and other emerging biopolymers
- **Raw Materials & Additives**: Bio-based additives, fillers, and reinforcing fibers
- **Processing Solutions**: Extrusion, injection molding, thermoforming, and advanced processing technologies
- **Application Opportunities**: Packaging, automotive, textiles, medical devices, electronics, and consumer goods
- **Testing & Labeling**: Standards, certifications, and compliance (ASTM, EN, ISO standards)
- **End-of-Life Options**: Composting, biodegradation, recycling, and circular economy strategies
- **Market Trends**: Supply and demand analysis, pricing trends, and growth opportunities
- **Regulatory Landscape**: US and North American regulatory developments and policy updates

## Conference Format
