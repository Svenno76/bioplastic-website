## Event Overview

The Circular Packaging & Biopolymer Summit 2026 is a focused, high-level industry gathering dedicated to advancing circular economy principles in packaging through biopolymer innovation and sustainable solutions. The summit brings together decision-makers, innovators, and industry leaders from across the biopolymer and packaging value chains.
