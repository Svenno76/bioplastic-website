## Event Overview

IPF Japan 2026 is the 11th edition of Asia's largest and most important plastics and rubber industry exhibition. Held triennially (every 3 years) since 1994, IPF Japan brings together the entire Asian plastics and rubber industries with global participation, showcasing cutting-edge technologies and innovations.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Trade Fair & Exhibition
- **Date**: December 1-5, 2026
- **Location**: Makuhari Messe, Chiba/Tokyo, Japan
- **Edition**: 11th edition (established 1994, held triennially)
- **Website**: [IPF Japan](https://www.ipfjapan.jp/english/)
- **Expected Attendance**: 45,000+ visitors
- **Frequency**: Every 3 years (triennial)

## Exhibition Focus Areas

- **Plastic Processing Machinery**: Injection molding, extrusion, thermoforming, blow molding equipment
- **Rubber Processing Technology**: Tire manufacturing, rubber products, elastomer processing
- **Materials Innovation**: Conventional and bioplastics, advanced polymers, compounds
- **Manufacturing Technologies**: Industry 4.0, automation, digital solutions
- **Processing Aids & Additives**: Colorants, reinforcing agents, processing aids
- **Quality Control & Testing**: Inspection equipment, testing devices, measurement systems
- **Tooling & Molds**: Injection molds, dies, tooling solutions
- **Recycling Technologies**: Plastic recycling equipment, material recovery systems
