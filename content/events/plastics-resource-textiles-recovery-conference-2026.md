## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Plastics Recycling Technologies**: Mechanical and chemical recycling processes, advanced sorting and separation methods, quality improvements for recycled materials
- **Minimum PCR Mandates & Compliance**: Regulatory requirements, industry standards, implementation strategies for brand owners
- **Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)**: EPR system design and implementation, compliance approaches, market impacts
- **Automotive Recycling**: End-of-life vehicle processing, material recovery from automotive plastics, design for recycling principles
- **Textile Recycling**: Fiber separation, polyester recovery, blended material challenges, fashion industry initiatives
- **Circular Economy Strategies**: Business models for circular economy, infrastructure development, supply chain integration
- **Material Recovery Quality**: Contamination reduction, quality standards, market demands for recycled materials
- **Policy & Regulation**: State and federal regulations, international standards, sustainability mandates
