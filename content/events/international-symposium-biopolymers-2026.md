## Event Overview

The International Symposium on Biopolymers (ISBP) 2026 is the 20th edition of this prestigious biennial event that has been bringing together biopolymer researchers and innovators since 1988. ISBP is the leading forum for presenting and discussing cutting-edge research in biopolymer science, engineering, and applications.

## Key Details

- **Event Type**: International Research Symposium
- **Date**: September 6-9, 2026
- **Location**: Dublin, Ireland
- **Edition**: 20th edition (established 1988)
- **Website**: [ISBP 2026](https://www.ucd.ie/sbbs/isbp2026/)
- **Frequency**: Biennial (held every 2 years)

## Topics & Focus Areas

- **Polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA)**: Production, properties, applications, and biodegradation
- **Polylactic Acid (PLA)**: Processing, performance enhancement, recycling strategies
- **Cellulose & Derived Polymers**: Modified cellulose, cellulose acetate, derivative properties
- **Pullulan & Other Biopolymers**: Properties, production methods, industrial applications
- **Cyanophycin**: Bacterial production, properties, potential applications
- **Natural Polyisoprenes**: Rubber, latex, and bio-based elastomers
- **Metabolic Pathways & Engineering**: Genetic engineering for biopolymer production, fermentation strategies
- **Functional Biopolymers**: Specialized properties, novel applications, performance improvements
- **Material Science & Engineering**: Blending, compositing, polymer modification, processing techniques
- **Biodegradation & Recycling**: Degradation mechanisms, recycling technologies, standardization
- **Sustainable Feedstocks**: Renewable sources, agricultural residues, waste-based production
- **Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)**: Environmental impact assessment, carbon footprint analysis
- **Industrial Applications**: Commercial scale production, market development

## Symposium Format

- **Oral Presentations**: Peer-reviewed research presentations by academics and industry researchers
- **Poster Sessions**: Research posters with direct author interaction
- **Keynote Lectures**: Leading researchers presenting state-of-the-art advances
- **Panel Discussions**: Expert discussions on emerging topics and challenges
- **Workshops**: Hands-on learning sessions on specific techniques
- **Networking Events**: Opportunities to connect with international research community
- **Exhibition**: Research equipment and service providers
