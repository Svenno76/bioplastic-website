## Event Overview

The International Conference on Bioplastics, Biocomposites and Biorefining (ICBBB) 2026 is a premier academic and industry conference dedicated to cutting-edge research and developments in bioplastics, biocomposites, and biorefining technologies. The conference attracts researchers, scientists, and industry professionals working on sustainable materials.
