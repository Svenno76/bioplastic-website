## Overview

Primary feedstock for PLA production via corn starch → glucose → lactic acid fermentation. US is dominant producer. Concerns: food vs fuel, land use, fertilizer runoff, GMO. Alternative: wheat, cassava, potato starch. 2G pathways use corn stover (residue) to avoid food competition.

## Key Concepts

Corn starch is the main PLA feedstock — US dominates; corn stover enables 2G routes avoiding food competition.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects

NatureWorks, Total Corbion, Corbion, ADM, Cargill, Ingredion, Tate & Lyle, Roquette, COFCO, Futerro are actively developing corn feedstock technologies and applications.

## Applications

Corn starch → Glucose → Lactic acid → PLA, Corn stover → 2G ethanol/chemicals, HFCS, Starch derivatives
