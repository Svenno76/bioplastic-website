## Overview

**Purocel™** is a proprietary portfolio of **catalysts and adsorbents** developed by **Evonik Industries** for the purification of pyrolysis oil derived from mixed plastic waste. The technology addresses the critical bottleneck in chemical recycling: converting heterogeneous, contaminated pyrolysis oil into a consistent, high-quality feedstock suitable for steam crackers.

## The Problem: Pyrolysis Oil Contamination

Pyrolysis oil from real-world plastic waste streams contains problematic impurities that prevent direct use in steam crackers:

| Contaminant | Source | Impact on Steam Cracker |
|-------------|--------|------------------------|
| **Chlorides (Cl)** | PVC, flame retardants | Corrosion, catalyst poisoning |
| **Metals (Na, K, Ca, Fe, Zn)** | Additives, fillers | Fouling, catalyst deactivation |
| **Nitrogen compounds** | Nylon, polyurethanes | Ammonia formation, NOx emissions |
| **Oxygenates** | PET, polyesters | Instability, gum formation |
| **Silicon** | Fillers, adhesives | Refractory deposits |
| **Dienes/olefins** | Thermal cracking products | Gum formation, fouling |

Typical steam cracker specifications require: **<1 ppm chlorides, <0.5 ppm metals, <50 ppm nitrogen**.

## The Purocel™ Solution

Evonik's Purocel™ series provides a **modular, multi-stage purification train** combining hydrotreating catalysts and fixed-bed adsorbents:

### Purocel™ Hydrotreating Catalysts (Catalytic Upgrading)

| Product | Function | Key Features |
|---------|----------|--------------|
| **Purocel HTC-1** | Hydrodechlorination, hydrogenation | Converts organic Cl to HCl; saturates dienes/olefins |
| **Purocel HTC-2** | Hydrodenitrogenation (HDN), hydrodesulfurization (HDS) | Deep N/S removal; stable under high contaminant loads |
| **Purocel HTC-3** | Guard catalyst | Protects downstream catalysts from residual metals/silicon |

### Purocel™ Adsorbents (Fixed-Bed Polishing)

| Product | Target Contaminants | Key Features |
|---------|---------------------|--------------|
| **Purocel 505** | Chlorides (HCl, organic Cl) | High-capacity chloride scavenging, regenerable |
| **Purocel 510** | Metals (Na, K, Ca, Fe, Zn) | Selective metal chelation, regenerable |
| **Purocel 515** | Silicon, phosphorus, arsenic | Specialty adsorbent for refractory elements |

### Integrated Process Flow

```
Pyrolysis Oil 
→ [Purocel HTC-1: Hydrodechlorination/Hydrogenation]
→ [Purocel 505: Chloride Polishing]
→ [Purocel HTC-2: HDN/HDS]
→ [Purocel 510/515: Metal/Si Polishing]
→ Steam Cracker-Ready Feedstock
```

**Achievable Purity:**
- Chlorides: **<0.5 ppm** (from 100–5,000 ppm typical)
- Total metals: **<0.1 ppm** (from 10–500 ppm typical)
- Nitrogen: **<10 ppm** (from 500–3,000 ppm typical)
- Olefins/dienes: Saturated for stability

## Technical Differentiation

1. **Synergistic Design** – Catalysts and adsorbents co-developed to work in sequence; catalysts convert reactive contaminants into forms adsorbents can capture.
2. **Regenerability** – Purocel 505 and 510 designed for thermal regeneration *in situ*, enabling cyclic operation without continuous consumable replacement.
3. **Feedstock Flexibility** – Modular design accommodates varying waste compositions (packaging, automotive shredder residue, WEEE, agricultural films).
4. **Drop-in Compatibility** – Fits standard fixed-bed hydrotreating reactors and adsorption vessels; no proprietary reactor technology required.
