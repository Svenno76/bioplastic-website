## Overview

Bioplastics in everyday products: electronics casings, toys, cosmetics packaging, household goods, footwear, personal care. Brands adopt for sustainability marketing and regulation compliance. Materials: PLA, Bio-PE, Bio-PET, PHA, PBAT blends. Often combined with recycled content.

## Key Concepts

Consumer brands adopt bioplastics for sustainability positioning — electronics, toys, cosmetics, household, footwear growing segments.
