## Overview

Biodegradable mulch films, silage films, and controlled-release fertilizer coatings that can be tilled into soil after use. Eliminates plastic retrieval and disposal. Key materials: PBAT blends, PLA, starch-based, PHA. Certified to EN 17033 / ISO 23517 for soil biodegradability.

## Key Concepts

Agricultural films that biodegrade in soil eliminate retrieval costs and plastic pollution — certified to EN 17033.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects

Novamont, BASF, Kingfa, BioBag, RKW, AB Rani Plast, Polifilm, Groupe Barbier, Agriplast are actively developing agricultural films technologies and applications.

Mulch films, Silage films, Greenhouse films, Controlled-release fertilizers, Seed coatings, Plant pots
