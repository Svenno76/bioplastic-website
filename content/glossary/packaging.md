## Overview

Largest market segment for bioplastics (~48% of market). Includes rigid packaging (bottles, trays, clamshells), flexible films, food service items, and barrier coatings. Driven by EU PPWR recycled content mandates, SUPD bans, and brand sustainability commitments.

## Key Concepts

Packaging drives bioplastics demand — regulation (PPWR, SUPD) and brand commitments are key growth drivers.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects
