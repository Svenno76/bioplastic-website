## Overview

Chemical recycling process that breaks polymers back into their original monomers through solvolysis (glycolysis, methanolysis, hydrolysis) or enzymatic action. Enables true circularity for condensation polymers like PET, PA, and PU.

## Key Concepts

Depolymerization reverses polymerization to recover pure monomers for virgin-quality recycled polymers.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects

Carbios, Loop Industries, Gramex, Ioniqa, Eastman, Indorama, JEPLAN, Jeplan are actively developing depolymerization technologies and applications.
