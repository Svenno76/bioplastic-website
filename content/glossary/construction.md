## Overview

Bioplastics in building materials: insulation foams (PHA, PLA), piping (Bio-PE), composites (natural fiber reinforced), coatings, adhesives. Driven by green building certifications (LEED, BREEAM, DGNB) and embodied carbon reduction. Still early stage vs packaging/automotive.

## Key Concepts

Construction adopts bioplastics for green building credits and embodied carbon reduction — insulation, piping, composites emerging.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects

BASF, Covestro, NatureWorks, Total Corbion, Kingfa, Technoform, Isocell, Gutex, Pavatex, HempFlax are actively developing construction applications technologies and applications.

## Applications
