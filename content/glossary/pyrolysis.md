## Overview

Thermal decomposition of plastics in the absence of oxygen (400-800°C) to produce pyrolysis oil, gas, and char. The oil can be used as feedstock for new plastics production, enabling chemical recycling of mixed or contaminated plastic waste.

## Key Concepts

Pyrolysis converts plastic waste into pyrolysis oil for new plastic production via chemical recycling.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects
