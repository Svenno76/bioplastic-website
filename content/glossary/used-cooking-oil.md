## Overview

Waste lipid feedstock for HVO/HEFA renewable diesel, SAF, and bio-based chemicals (azelaic acid, sebacic acid, polymer precursors). Major circular feedstock — diverts waste from sewage, avoids land use. EU counts double toward renewable targets. Supply limited, fraud risk (virgin palm oil mislabeled as UCO).

## Key Concepts

Used cooking oil is a premium circular feedstock for renewable fuels and bio-chemicals — waste-to-value, double counting in EU.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects

Neste, Diamond Green Diesel, ENI, TotalEnergies, Preem, Renewable Energy Group, Greenergy, Olleco, Valley Proteins are actively developing used cooking oil (uco) technologies and applications.
