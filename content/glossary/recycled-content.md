## Overview

Proportion of post-consumer or post-industrial recycled material in a product. Key metric for circular economy compliance, mandated by EU PPWR (30% recycled content in plastic packaging by 2030). Verified through chain-of-custody certification (ISCC PLUS, RSB).

## Key Concepts

Recycled content mandates drive demand for recycled plastics — verified by certification to prevent greenwashing.

## Industry Landscape

### Major Companies & Projects

Veolia, Suez, Biffa, Viridor, ALPLA, Greiner, Berry Global, Amcor, Sealed Air are actively developing recycled content technologies and applications.
