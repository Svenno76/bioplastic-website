## About Bioplastics Portal

**Bioplastics Portal** is a comprehensive information resource dedicated to the bioplastics industry. Our mission is to aggregate and organize publicly available information about bioplastics manufacturers, products, technologies, and market developments in one accessible place.

### What We Cover

- **Company Profiles** — Detailed profiles of bioplastics producers, converters, compounders, and additive manufacturers worldwide
- **Industry News** — Latest developments, partnerships, product launches, and market movements
- **Blog** — In-depth analysis, research insights, and expert perspectives on bioplastics innovation
- **Market Studies** — Research reports, forecasts, and technical analysis
- **Events** — Conferences, trade shows, workshops, and industry gatherings
- **Glossary** — Comprehensive definitions of bioplastics terminology, from basic concepts to advanced material science

### About the Author

**Sven Cammerer** is the creator and maintainer of Bioplastics Portal. Based in Switzerland, he combines technical expertise with a passion for sustainable materials and the bioplastics industry.

### Data Sources

All information on this site is gathered from publicly available sources including company websites, press releases, industry publications, and trade organizations. Company profiles link directly to official sources where possible.

### Contact

For corrections, updates, or suggestions, please reach out via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Svenno76/bioplastic-website/issues).

### Technical

This site is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) and deployed on [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/). Content is managed through a combination of Markdown files and structured data. The source code is open source and available on [GitHub](https://github.com/Svenno76/bioplastic-website).
