## About Bioplastics Portal

**Bioplastics Portal** is a comprehensive information resource dedicated to the bioplastics industry. Our mission is to aggregate and organize publicly available information about bioplastics manufacturers, products, technologies, and market developments in one accessible place.

Since its launch, the portal has grown to cover **1,235 pages** including **188 company profiles**, industry news, a detailed bioplastics glossary, market studies, and educational guides — making it one of the most comprehensive free resources for bioplastics information on the web.

## About the Author

**Sven Cammerer** is the creator, editor, and maintainer of Bioplastics Portal. Based in Switzerland, he is a software engineer and bioplastics industry analyst with years of experience tracking developments across the bioplastics value chain — from feedstock and polymer production to end-of-life solutions and market dynamics.

Sven is the author of *Get Cited or Get Left Behind*, a practical guide to Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) — helping content creators and businesses ensure their expertise is visible in AI-powered search results. His work bridges the gap between technical bioplastics knowledge and modern digital visibility strategies.

### Expertise

- **Bioplastics industry analysis** — Tracking 188+ companies across PLA, PHA, PBAT, bio-PE, and other material categories
- **AEO/GEO optimization** — Structured data, JSON-LD, and AI-search citation strategies

## Data Sources & Editorial Standards

All information on this site is gathered from publicly available sources including company websites, press releases, industry publications, and trade organizations. Company profiles link directly to official sources where possible.

Content is reviewed for accuracy and updated regularly as new information becomes available. If you spot an error or have updated information about a listed company, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/Svenno76/bioplastic-website/issues).
