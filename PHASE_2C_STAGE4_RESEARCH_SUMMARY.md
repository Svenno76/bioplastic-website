   - Company Type: Compounder
   - Focus: Biodegradable polymer processing and compounding
   - Technology: Injection molding, thermoforming, extrusion, 3D printing
   - Materials: PLA, PHA, biodegradable plastics, recycled materials
   - Employees: 4-50
   - International: Yes (70% export sales)
   - Significance: Regional plastics processor transitioning to bioplastics

2. **C2C** (Lublin)
   - Company Type: Bioplastic Producer
   - Focus: Fully compostable biodegradable packaging from corn starch
   - Technology: Bacteriostatic layer innovation, oxobiodegradable formulations
   - Materials: Corn starch, PLA, PBAT, natural composites
   - Employees: 50-100
   - Certification: First Polish company with TÜV OK Compost Home certification
   - Significance: Commercial viability of bio-based packaging in Poland

### Czech Republic (2 companies identified)

   - Company Type: Technology Company
   - Focus: PHA bioplastics from waste cooking oil (Hydal technology)
   - Technology: Fermentation-based PHA production, proprietary process
   - Materials: PHA, P3HB, nanofibers, waste-valorization based
   - Employees: 10-15
   - Recognition: Frost & Sullivan Award 2015, Eastern European Business Elite Award 2016
   - Significance: Research-driven biotech innovator, waste valorization focus

2. **Brno University of Technology** (Brno)
   - Company Type: University
   - Focus: Materials research, biopolymer development, testing
   - Technology: Advanced characterization, composting simulation, materials testing
   - Materials: Biopolymers, biocomposites, PLA, PHA blends
   - Employees: 1000+ (university total)
   - Infrastructure: Materials Research Centre, dedicated bioplastics lab
   - Significance: Regional research hub, technology commercialization partner

### Hungary (1 company identified)
